Figure 4. Signaling pathways in Lkb1 mutant SCC. From: LKB1 deficiency sensitizes mice to carcinogen-induced tumorigenesis.

A) RAF pulldown assay showing levels of activated Ras-GTP in normal skin (lane 1), SCC from from Lkb1+/- mice (lanes 2 and 3), and papillomas (lanes 4 and 5). B) Western blot analysis of ERK and PI3K pathway components in papillomas (lanes 5 and 6) and Lkb1-mutant SCC (lanes 1-4). C) Western blot analysis of the AMPK/mTOR signaling pathway in papillomas (lanes 5 and 6) and SCC (lanes 1-4).
