Figure 5. From: Knockdown of FABP3 Impairs Cardiac Development in Zebrafish through the Retinoic Acid Signaling Pathway.

FABP3-MO alters the retinoic acid signaling pathway. Raldh2 expression was not significantly different in FABP3-MO injected zebrafish, as visualized by in situ hybridization at 14 hpf (B) and 24 hpf (D), compared with Wt controls at 14 hpf (A) and 24 hpf (C), respectively. Stable expression levels of Raldh2 were also confirmed by real-time PCR (E). Cyp26a1 expression was significantly upregulated in FABP3-MO injected zebrafish, as visualized by in situ hybridization at 14 hpf (G) and 24 hpf (I), compared with Wt controls at 14 hpf (F) and 24 hpf (H), respectively. Increased expression levels of Cyp26a1 were confirmed by real-time PCR (J).* p < 0.05.
