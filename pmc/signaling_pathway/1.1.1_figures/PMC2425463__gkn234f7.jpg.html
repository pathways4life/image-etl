Figure 7. From: Enhanced identification and biological validation of differential gene expression via Illumina whole-genome expression arrays through the use of the model-based background correction methodology.

Western blot of proteins involved in the Tnfr2 and NF-kappaB signaling pathway. The figures indicate upregulation of Tnfr2 and NF-kappaB, and downregulation of Ikk. These results were consistent with gene-expression analysis using the MBCB data set and suggest active involvement of this pathway in mouse AML samples.
