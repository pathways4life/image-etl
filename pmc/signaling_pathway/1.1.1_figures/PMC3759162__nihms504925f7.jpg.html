Fig. 7. From: NSBP-1 mediates the effects of cholesterol on insulin/IGF-1 signaling in Caenorhabditis elegans.

Proposed model for the potential role of NSBP-1 in the insulin pathway. DAF-2 signals through the AGE-1 signaling cascade activate AKT-1 by phosphorylation. These kinases in turn regulate DAF-16 and NSBP-1 negatively. In the absence of insulin signaling, NSBP-1 binds to DAF-16 in the nucleus to regulate insulin signaling-mediated dauer formation, fat storage, and oxidative stress
