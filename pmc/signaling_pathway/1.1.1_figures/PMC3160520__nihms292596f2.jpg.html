Fig. 2. From: Apolipoprotein E reduces food intake via PI3K/Akt signaling pathway in the hypothalamus.

Inhibition of apoE-induced activation of pAkt by LY294002 (50 μM), an inhibitor of the PI3K signaling pathway, in cultured hypothalamic cells. A, representative immunoblots of pAkt and total Akt; B, quantitative analysis. Data represent mean ± SEM; n = 3. Bars that do not share the same letter are significantly different from each other.
