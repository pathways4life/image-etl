Figure 9. From: Hedgehog Pathway Blockade Inhibits Melanoma Cell Growth in Vitro and in Vivo.

Ingenuity Canonical Pathway analysis reveals downregulation of stem cell pluripotency signaling and cell cycle regulators. (A–D) Downregulated IPA (Ingenuity Pathway Analysis) canonical pathways are shown for A375 and WM3248 cell lines treated with either 5 µM NVP-LDE-225 or SMO siRNA. The transcriptional profiles of the DMSO treated and non-targeting siRNA treated A375 and WM3248 cells were used as controls in the IPA analysis. The stem cell pathways that were downregulated by both SMO siRNA and NVP-LDE-225 in both cell lines are marked with an asterisk (*).
