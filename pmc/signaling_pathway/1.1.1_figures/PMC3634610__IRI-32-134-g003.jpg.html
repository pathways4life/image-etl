Figure 3. From: C-Type Lectin-Like Receptors of the Dectin-1 Cluster: Ligands and Signaling Pathways.

ITAM-like-bearing CTLRs within the Dectin-1 cluster. As well as Dectin-1, there are two additional receptors that contain ITAM-like motifs. These receptors share Syk as a primary downstream signaling kinase; however, the lack of a tri-acidic in CLEC-9A and the presence of an isoleucine prevent the endocytic and activation functions of this receptor. CLEC-2 utilises its tri-acidic motif to complement activation through Syk resulting in adhesion and clot formation in platelets. Dashed arrows represent a pathway that has yet to be fully defined.
