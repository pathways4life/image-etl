Fig. 5. From: A direct cross-talk between interferon-? and sonic hedgehog signaling that leads to the proliferation of neuronal precursor cells.

Attenuation of IFN-γ-induced GNP proliferation by inhibition of Shh or Shh signaling pathway. The primary GNP cultures were treated with cyclopamine (CPM) (A) or anti-Shh antibodies (B) at the indicated concentrations before the addition of IFN-γ (200 IU/ml), and MTT proliferation assay was performed after 72-hour treatment. (n = 3-4; *: p < 0.05; **: p < 0.01 (one-way ANOVA followed by Dunnett's t-test)).
