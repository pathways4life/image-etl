Figure 3. From: Up-Regulation of Rhoa/Rho Kinase Pathway by Translationally Controlled Tumor Protein in Vascular Smooth Muscle Cells.

Role of TCTP in the RhoA-mediated signaling pathway in TCTP+/− mice. Representative photomicrographs indicating immunoreactivity of TCTP, RhoA, and p-MLC in the sections of aortic tissues from WT and TCTP+/− mice. IHC was performed using anti-TCTP, -RhoA, and p-MLC antibodies (N = 4–5).
