Figure 3. From: Integrative Survival-Based Molecular Profiling of Human Pancreatic Cancer.

Pathway based analysis of high ranking composite score genes reveals key signaling pathways associated with PDAC clinical progression. The diagram depicts putative interactions of highly ranked poor (red) and good (green) prognosis-associated genes from the composite signature in relation to SRC signaling or the PI3K/AKT/mTOR pathway. Genes not highlighted in green or red are implied.
