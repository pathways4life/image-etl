Figure 3. From: SUMOylation in Drosophila Development.

Homeodomain interacting protein kinase (Hipk) SUMOylation may down-regulate c-Jun N-terminal kinase (JNK) signaling. Phosphorylation of JNK in the cytoplasm by Hipk may stimulate JNK pathway activity. SUMOylation of Hipk may interfere with JNK signaling by sequestering Hipk in the nucleus.
