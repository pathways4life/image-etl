Figure 4. From: BayMiR: inferring evidence for endogenous miRNA-induced gene repression from mRNA expression profiles.

WNT signaling pathway. WNT signaling pathway: 32 targets of 5 miRNAs are involved in the pathway (red boxes). 14 mRNAs are targeted by the remaining miRNAs are colored in yellow; and 23 mRNAs involved in the pathway were excluded from the BayMiR target list since their expression variabilities across arrays were very low (white boxes). The miRNA family IDs: miR-518a-5p/520d-5p/524-5p,miR-556-3p,miR-4514/4692,miR-548aeajamx,miR-135ab/135a-5p;.
