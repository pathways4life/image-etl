Figure 10. Model of a Wnt/Tnc/Pdgfr signaling axis regulating lung SMC development.. From: Wnt signaling regulates smooth muscle precursor development in the mouse lung via a tenascin C/PDGFR pathway.

Airway epithelium (green) expresses Wnt7b, which acts on mesenchymal SMC precursors (yellow). Wnt7b and Î²-catenin signaling directly activates Tnc expression and in turn promotes airway SMC (blue) and VSMC (red) precursor proliferation and development.
