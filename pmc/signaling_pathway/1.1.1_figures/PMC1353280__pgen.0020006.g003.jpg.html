Figure 3. An Expression Quantitative Trait Locus on Chromosome 2 Regulates Many GPCR Protein Pathway Genes. From: Combined Expression Trait Correlations and Expression Quantitative Trait Locus Mapping.

(A) Comapping of GPCR pathway traits to loci on Chromosome 2. The figure shows the peaks on Chromosome 2 versus LOD score for 174 GPCR protein signaling pathway traits. The closed circles correspond to 38 seed traits.(B) Comapping of GPCR pathway traits. The figure shows the magnitude (y-axis) versus location of LOD peaks (x-axis is scaled by cM) for 174 GPCR protein signaling pathway traits. The closed circles correspond to the 38 seed traits. The interval mapping LOD profiles for all 38 seeds are included in Figure S2.
