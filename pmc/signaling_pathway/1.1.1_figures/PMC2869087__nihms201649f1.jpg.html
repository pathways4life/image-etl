Figure 1. Hedgehog proangiogenic signaling pathway. From: Hedgehog takes a new "RHOad" to angiogenesis.

Schematic representation of this novel proangiogenic Hedgehog signaling cascade in endothelial cells. Hedgehog promotes tube formation through a SMOOTHENED-Gi-RAC1-RHOA pathway. This cellular event is GLI independent and is accompanied by ligand dependent inhibition of PATCHED pro-apoptotic functions in endothelial cells.
