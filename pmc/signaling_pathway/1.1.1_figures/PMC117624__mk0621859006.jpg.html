Figure 6. From: Calmodulin Regulates Intracellular Trafficking of Epidermal Growth Factor Receptor and the MAPK Signaling Pathway.

W-13 effects on MAPK signaling pathway. Dissection of the MAPK signaling pathway. Serum starved COS-1 cells grown in 100-mm dishes were incubated with W-13 (10 μg/ml) for 60 min, and the last 15 min with the indicated concentrations of EGF, at 37°C. Lysates with equal amount of protein were electrophoresed and the stimulation of EGFR-phosphotyrosine (RC-20) (A), the Ras activation (Ras-GTP) assessed by GST-RBD pull down (B), and the MEK activation (C) were analyzed by Western blotting.
