Figure 1. Components of the Hippo signaling pathway are expressed in hematopoietic stem and progenitor cells.. From: Normal Hematopoietic Stem Cell Function in Mice with Enforced Expression of the Hippo Signaling Effector YAP1.

mRNA expression levels of upstreams components of the Hippo pathway (A) and the downstream effector Yap1 (B) in different stem and progenitor compartments. Values are normalized against Hprt1 for n = 2 (A) and n = 3 (B). Error bars represent SEM. Abbreviations: LT, long-term HSCs; ST, short-term HSCs; LMPPs, lymphoid myeloid primed progenitor; Lin+, lineage positive.
