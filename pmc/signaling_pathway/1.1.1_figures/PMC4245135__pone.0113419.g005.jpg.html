Figure 5. Signaling pathway downstream of inflammatory mediator (TNF-α) signaling in human DPSC.. From: Chronic Inflammation and Angiogenic Signaling Axis Impairs Differentiation of Dental-Pulp Stem Cells.

Prolonged exposure to TNF-α induce NF-κB signaling axis, which in turn induce an enhanced antiapoptotic gene expression and decreased telomere length. The resultant chromosomal instability leads to an increase in the proliferation of DPSC, with an impaired differentiation potential.
