(C) PI3K pathway-mutated HNSCC tumors have higher of non-synonymous mutations and (D) cancer gene mutations when compared to HNSCC tumors without any PI3K pathway mutations. Bar graph representing the average number of non-synonymous mutations per tumor (C) and the average number of cancer gene mutations (D) in 151 HNSCC tumors.
