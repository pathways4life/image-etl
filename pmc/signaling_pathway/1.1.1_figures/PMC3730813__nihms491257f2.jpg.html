Figure 2. PRRs share immune signaling pathways. From: The Role of Pattern Recognition Receptors in Intestinal Inflammation.

Most TLRs except for TLR3 induce NF-κB activation through the MyD88 pathway. TLR3 exclusively induces IRF3 activation through the TRIF pathway. The TRIF pathway is shared by TLR4 and TLR5. NOD1 and NOD2 also activate NF-κB via the RICK pathway. All TLRs and NODs also induce activate MAP-kinases.
