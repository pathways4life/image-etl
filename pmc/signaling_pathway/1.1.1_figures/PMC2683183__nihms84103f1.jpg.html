Figure 1. GPCR-mediated signaling to mitochondria. From: Cardioprotective Signaling to Mitochondria.

Occupation of the GPCR leads to activation of PI3-kinase, phosphorylation of phosphatidylinositol bisphosphate, and activation of the phosphatidylinositol-dependent kinases (PDKs) [128]. PDKs then phosphorylate Akt, which initiates the remainder of the cytosolic signaling pathway: endothelial nitric oxide synthase (eNOS) is phosphorylated, leading to production of NO.
