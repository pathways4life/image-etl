Figure 9. From: The Marine Fungal Metabolite, Dicitrinone B, Induces A375 Cell Apoptosis through the ROS-Related Caspase Pathway.

The proposed signaling pathways for dicitrinone B-induced apoptosis in A375 cells. The effect of apoptosis is most likely achieved through rapid ROS generation, which, at least in part, leads to mitochondrial membrane permeabilization and triggers the intrinsic apoptosis pathway, as well as the extrinsic apoptosis pathway.
