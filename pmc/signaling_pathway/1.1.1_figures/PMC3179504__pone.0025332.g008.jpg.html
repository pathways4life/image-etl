Figure 8. Intracellular signaling mechanisms involved in NK1R-mediated membrane blebbing.. From: Substance P Induces Rapid and Transient Membrane Blebbing in U373MG Cells in a p21-Activated Kinase-Dependent Manner.

Bleb formation mediated by the endogenous full-length NK1R in U373MG cells is dependent of PAK and is associated with tubulin depolymerization. The classic Gq-dependent signaling pathway triggered by NK1R is not involved in morphological changes induced by SP.
