Fig. 5. Parameter sensitivity analysis of the intracellular pathway model. From: Cytokine Combination Therapy Prediction for Bone Remodeling in Tissue Engineering Based on the Intracellular Signaling Pathway.

Above subfigure shows that the expression of Runx2 is more sensitivity to V6, K6 and d4. Below subfigure shows that the expression of Osx is more sensitivity to V8, K8 and d8, as well as to V1, K1, d1 and V7, K7.
