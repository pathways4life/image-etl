Figure 7. The proposed mechanisms by which nicotine promotes NPC cell proliferation.. From: Nicotine Promotes Proliferation of Human Nasopharyngeal Carcinoma Cells by Regulating ?7AChR, ERK, HIF-1? and VEGF/PEDF Signaling.

Nicotine promotes NPC cell proliferation by simultaneously promoting the α7AChR, ERK, HIF-1α and VEGF/PEDF signaling. Nicotine interacts with α7AChR on the surface of NPC cells, activates ERK/MAPK signaling pathway, upregulates HIF-1α expression, elevates VEGF/PEDF ratio, thereby promotes NPC cells proliferation.
