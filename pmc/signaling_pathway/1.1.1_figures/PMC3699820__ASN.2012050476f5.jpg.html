Figure 5. From: Rheb/mTORC1 Signaling Promotes Kidney Fibroblast Activation and Fibrosis.

Ectopic expression of Rheb promotes fibroblast activation. Primary cultured kidney interstitial fibroblasts are treated with rapamycin. (A) Activation of mTORC1 signaling in Rheb overexpression cells. (B) Semi-quantitative analysis of mTORC1 signaling pathway protein abundance in primary cultured fibroblasts. *P<0.05 compared with controls; #P<0.05 compared with TGFβ1-treated cells (n=3). (C) Elevation of FN and α-SMA protein expression level in Rheb overexpression fibroblasts. (D) Semi-quantitative analysis of FN and α-SMA protein expression in primary cultured fibroblasts. *P<0.05 compared with controls; #P<0.05 compared with TGFβ1-treated cells (n=3). (E) Representative immunofluorescence images showing FN and α-SMA expression in the primary cultured fibroblasts. Slides are counterstained with DAPI for nuclear visualization. DAPI, 4',6-diamidino-2-phenylindole; WT, wild type; Rapa, rapamycin.
