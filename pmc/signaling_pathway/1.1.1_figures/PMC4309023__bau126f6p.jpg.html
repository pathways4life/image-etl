Figure 6. From: Comparison of human cell signaling pathway databases—evolution, drawbacks and challenges.

Core Hedgehog pathway and its cross talks. Schematic shows the cross connection of the core Hedgehog pathway (taken from KEGG) with the other pathways and molecules [e.g. Parathyroid hormone (PTH) signaling pathway, proteasome pathway, Vitamin D3, Megalin and TGFβ2 molecules], which are considered in different databases, such as NCI-PID, CPDB, GENE GO and MILLIPORE. Hence, the number of pathway components of this pathway varies significantly within these databases.
