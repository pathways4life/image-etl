Figure 5. Injection of F13A suppresses the effects of apelin on glycogen synthesis and insulin signaling pathway in TNF-α-treated mice.. From: Apelin Ameliorates TNF-?-Induced Reduction of Glycogen Synthesis in the Hepatocytes through G Protein-Coupled Receptor APJ.

F13A (20 µg/mouse) or/and apelin 13 (20 nmol/kg) was injected in TNF-α-treated C57BL/6J mice. Injection of F13A suppressed the effects of apelin on glycogen synthesis (A) and insulin signaling pathway (B) in TNF-α-treated mice. Data represent the means ± S.E.M., n = 5 mice per group. * p<0.05; ** p<0.01 and *** p<0.001 by ANOVA test.
