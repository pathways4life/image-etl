Fig. 5. Wnt signaling pathway supports Jagged1 expression in ovarian cancer cells.. From: Jagged1 Expression Regulated by Notch3 and Wnt/?-catenin Signaling Pathways in Ovarian Cancer.

Quantitative real time PCR shows a significant decrease in Jagged1 expression level after knockdown of either Î²-catenin (left panel) or TCF4 (right panel) in OVCAR3 cells. Well-known WNT targets including CCND1, SOX9, and CD44 are used as positive controls.
