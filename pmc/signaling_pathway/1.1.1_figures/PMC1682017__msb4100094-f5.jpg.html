Figure 5. From: Effects of HER2 overexpression on cell signaling networks governing proliferation and migration.

Effect of HRG stimulation versus EGF stimulation in high HER2-expressing cells. Visualization of the fold change in phosphorylation level between HRG-stimulated and EGF-stimulated 24H cells provides a network view of the mechanistic effects underlying differential response to distinct growth factor stimuli on (A) the canonical EGFR signaling cascades and (B) the cell migration-associated signaling pathway.
