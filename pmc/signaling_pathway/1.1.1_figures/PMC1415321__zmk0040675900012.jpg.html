Figure 12. From: Cell Surface Transglutaminase Promotes RhoA Activation via Integrin Clustering and Suppression of the Src-p190RhoGAP Signaling Pathway.

Summary of nonenzymatic effects of cell surface tTG on RhoA activity. Activation of RhoA by integrin-associated tTG is mediated by formation of integrin clusters and down-regulation of the Src-p190RhoGAP signaling pathway. The light, gray, and dark ovals, rectangles, and squares for Src, p190RhoGAP, and RhoA reflect their relative activities in the order of increase.
