Figure 2. Q-PCR validation of the Wnt/β-catenin signaling pathway alterations in Iqgap2−/− livers.. From: Transcript Profiling Identifies Iqgap2−/− Mouse as a Model for Advanced Human Hepatocellular Carcinoma.

The same liver RNA samples used for microarray (24-month-old WT, N  =  3, and 24-month-old KO, N  =  3) were analyzed using the Mouse Wnt Signaling Pathway RT2 Profiler PCR Array. 11 genes belonging to the pathway and found to be differentially expressed in Iqgap2−/− livers are shown.
