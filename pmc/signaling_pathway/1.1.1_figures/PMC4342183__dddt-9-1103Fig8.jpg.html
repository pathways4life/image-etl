VEGF signaling pathway in the target list of hsa-miR-181a-5p based on miRTarBase 4.0.Notes: hsa-miR-181a-5p can regulate the VEGF signaling pathway. VEGF is an important signaling protein involved in both vasculogenesis and angiogenesis. All members of the VEGF family stimulate cellular responses by binding to tyrosine kinase receptors (the VEGFRs) on the cell surface, causing them to dimerize and become activated through transphosphorylation. This triggers a signaling cascade that activates several signaling pathways, such as PI3K/Akt, Erk1/2, Smad, and Notch, and results in endothelial cell proliferation and migration.
