FIGURE 2. From: Dentin Matrix Protein 1 (DMP1) Signals via Cell Surface Integrin.

DMP1 activates signaling through the MAPK pathway in hMSC cells. hMSC cells were first treated with DMP1, then analyzed by Western blot for the activation of known components of the MAPK signaling cascade. a, sharp increases in phosphorylated JNK, ERK, and MEK were detected in response to rDMP1F treatment. b, the activation of ERK by rDMP1 signaling can be effectively blocked with the ERK-specific inhibitor, U0126.
