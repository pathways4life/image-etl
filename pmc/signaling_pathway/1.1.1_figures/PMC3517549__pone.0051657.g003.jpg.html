Pathway position, protein length, and connectivity were treated as exogenous variables, while the rest were treated as endogenous variables. The causal dependencies between variables assumed in the model are represented by single-headed arrows. Correlations between exogenous variables are represented by double-headed arrows. The numbers on the arrows represent the standardized path coefficients (β). Solid and broken lines represent significant and nonsignificant relationships, respectively.
