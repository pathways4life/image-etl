Figure 3. From: Genome-wide analysis of murine bone marrow-derived very small embryonic-like stem cells reveals that mitogenic growth factor signaling pathways play a crucial role in the quiescence and ageing of these cells.

Overlay of the insulin-like growth factor-1 (IGF-1) signaling pathway with microarray data for comparison of very small embryonic-like stem cells (VSELs) with hematopoietic stem cells (HSCs). Relative expression is depicted as fold-change in gene expression. Fold-changes in gene expression between VSELs and HSCs are shown below the indicated genes. The canonical IGF-1 signaling pathway was built using ingenuity pathway analysis (IPA) software version 8.7.
