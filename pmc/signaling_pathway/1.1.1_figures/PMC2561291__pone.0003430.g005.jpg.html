Figure 5. Schematic representation of Signaling Flux Redistribution (SFR).. From: Signaling Flux Redistribution at Toll-Like Receptor Pathway Junctions.

(A) removal of MyD88 results in enhancement of TRAM-dependant pathway, (B) removal of TRAF6 results in enhancement of TRAM-dependant pathway downstream of TRIF, (C) overexpression of MyD88 downregulates TRAM-dependant pathway, (D) removal of TRAM does not enhance the MyD88-dependant pathway due to upstream intermediates. * signaling molecules/events upstream of TRAM [9], [25], [26].
