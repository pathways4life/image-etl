Figure 8. A hypothesized signal pathway that Mtmr8 regulates zebrafish muscle development.. From: Cooperation of Mtmr8 with PI3K Regulates Actin Filament Modeling and Muscle Development in Zebrafish.

Knockdown of Mtmr8 promotes PI3K/Akt pathway and prevents Hh signaling pathway. The two pathways may interact with each other in regulating F-actin modeling and muscle development.
