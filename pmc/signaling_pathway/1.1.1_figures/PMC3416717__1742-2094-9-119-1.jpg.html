Figure 1. From: Immunodominant fragments of myelin basic protein initiate T cell-dependent pain.

T-helper cell signaling is induced after CCI. Ingenuity pathway analysis of the canonical pathways (A) and signaling cascades (B) using the proteins unique to sciatic nerve proteome at day 7 after CCI or sham operation. The bars represent -log (P value) for a system or a pathway to be represented with a threshold set at 1.4 (P < 0.05) in n = 3/group. CCI presents as an infectious disease, with CD28 Th cell signaling and T cell apoptosis among unique pathways activated relative to sham-operated nerve (asterisks).
