Figure 4. The GG does not express pGC-D. From: The Grueneberg Ganglion olfactory subsystem employs a cGMP signaling pathway.

A) Pde2a immunolabeling in thick sections from the olfactory neuroepithelium of a transgenic GCD-iTG mouse. GFP-positive GC-D neurons (green) express Pde2a (magenta), consistent with their use of a cGMP signaling pathway. B) Pde2a-positive GG cells (magenta) in thick sections from the nasal vestibule are devoid of GFP (green) and, hence, pGC-D. Scale bars: 60 Î¼m.
