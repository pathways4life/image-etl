Figure 4. From: Activity-dependent CREB phosphorylation: Convergence of a fast, sensitive calmodulin kinase pathway and a slow, less sensitive mitogen-activated protein kinase pathway.

Differential recruitment of the MAPK signaling pathway. (A) Dynamics of [Ca2+]i generated by mild (20 mM K+) or strong (90 mM K+) depolarization. Traces represent somatic [Ca2+]i measured with fura-2 ratiometric imaging. (B) Dynamics of pMAPK generated by mild (20 mM K+) or strong (90 mM K+) depolarization, determined with an antibody specific for pMAPK. (C) Dynamics of CREB phosphorylation generated by mild (20 mM K+) or strong (90 mM K+) depolarization.
