Figure 4. PI3K signaling pathway activation protected cells from apoptosis induced by combination treatment of LY294002 and tamoxifen.. From: Sensitization of Glioma Cells to Tamoxifen-Induced Apoptosis by Pl3-Kinase Inhibitor through the GSK-3?/?-Catenin Signaling Pathway.

U251 cells were treated with 10 µmol/L LY294002 (LY), or/and 5 µmol/L tamoxifen (TAM), or combination of LY and TAM with additional IGF-1 (50 ng/ml) for 12 h, followed by Annexin V-FITC/propidium iodide staining. Columns representing the flow cytometry data presented in left. Data are means ±S.E.M. of values from three independent experiments; FL2-H, propidium iodide; FL1-H, Annexin V.
