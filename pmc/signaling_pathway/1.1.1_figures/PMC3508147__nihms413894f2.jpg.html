Figure 2. Activation of stress response pathways suppresses germ cell loss in response to DGLA feeding. From: Stress response pathways protect germ cells from omega-6 polyunsaturated fatty acid-mediated toxicity in C. elegans.

A) Schematic of the conserved insulin/IGF-1 and TGF-b signaling pathways in C. elegans. B, C, and D) Mutations in the insulin signaling pathway and the TGF-b signaling pathway modulate the sterility responses. Graphs display the percentage of worms in a population that are sterile sterility fed on the various concentrations of DGLA. Error bars, S.E.M, *p≤0.05, **p≤0.01, determined by two tailed Student’s t-test.
