Fig 1. From: The TLR4-TRIF Pathway Protects against H5N1 Influenza Virus Infection.

Schematic diagram of the TLR4 signaling pathway. MD-2 and CD14 (green) are essential for both arms of the TLR4 signaling pathway, whereas MyD88 (purple) and TRIF (blue) are essential for signaling that leads to cytokine/chemokine or type I IFN production, respectively.
