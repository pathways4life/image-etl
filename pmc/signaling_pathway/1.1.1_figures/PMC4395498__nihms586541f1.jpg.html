Figure 1. Schematic of Akt signaling and regulation. From: Signaling Mechanisms Regulating Myelination in the Central Nervous System.

Extracellular ligand (purple) binding a transmembrane receptor and activating the PI3K/Akt pathway (blue icons). Lipid second messengers PIP2 and PIP3 indicated in yellow. Negative regulators indicated in red. Blunted arrows indicate inhibition. Dashed line indicates indirect regulation.
