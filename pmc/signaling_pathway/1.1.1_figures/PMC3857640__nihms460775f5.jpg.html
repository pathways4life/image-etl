Figure 5. Schematic presentation of signaling pathway involved in pre-osteoblast proliferation induced by MOS-J/POS-1 CMs. From: Conditioned media from mouse osteosarcoma cells promote MC3T3-E1 cell proliferation using JAKs and PI3-K/Akt signal crosstalk.

MOS-J/POS-1 conditioned media (CMs) induced novel JAKs (JAK1 and JAK2) and PI3-K/Akt signal crosstalk in osteoblastic cells. The pre-osteoblast pro-proliferating signal induced by MOS-J/POS-1 CMs was transmitted via JAKs/PI3-K/Akt/mTOR pathway. “Soluble factor(s)”: Pro-proliferating factor(s) of pre-osteoblast cell produced by osteosarcoma cells. Solid line arrow: The responsible signaling pathway for pre-osteoblast proliferation induced by MOS-J/POS-1 CMs. Dotted line arrow: MOS-J/POS-1 CMs-induced but non-responsible signaling pathway for pre-osteoblast proliferation.
