Figure 4. From: Effects of HER2 overexpression on cell signaling networks governing proliferation and migration.

Effect of increased HER2 expression on phosphorylation sites within the EGFR signaling network. Visualization of the fold change in phosphorylation level between 24H and parental cells stimulated with EGF provides a network view of the mechanistic effects of HER2 overexpression on (A) the canonical EGFR signaling cascades and (B) the cell migration-associated signaling pathway.
