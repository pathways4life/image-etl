FIG. 5. From: Transcriptional Regulation of the TATA-Binding Protein by Ras Cellular Signaling.

hTBP promoter activity is not regulated by the PI-3 kinase signaling pathway. Primary rat hepatocytes were transiently transfected with the following DNAs: A, 5.5 μg of p−4500/+66hTBP-luc together with 1.7 μg of RasV12 or RasV12-C40; B, 5.5 μg of c-fos promoter-luc together with 1.7 μg of RasV12 or RasV12-C40. Wortmannin (0.5 μM) was added where indicated to cells 14 h prior to harvesting of the transfected cells.
