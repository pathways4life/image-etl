Figure 5. From: Transcriptomic analysis of differentially expressed genes in the Ras1CA-overexpressed and wildtype posterior silk glands.

Verification of transcriptomic results of DEGs only in “pathways in cancer”, “insulin signaling pathway”, or “MAPK signaling pathway” by qPCR. DEGs only in “pathways in cancer” (A), “insulin signaling pathway” (B), “MAPK signaling pathway” (C). The transcriptional results of DEGs (FPKM) are marked with black. The q-PCR data of DEGs upregulated and downregulated by Ras1 are marked with red and green. The mis-matched DEGs are marked with gray. WT: wildtype; Ras1: Fil-GAL4 > UAS-Ras1CA.
