Fig. 4. OTEs and off-targets of raloxifene, tamoxifen, paclitaxel, and fulvestrant on the cell cycle G1/S checkpoint and P53 signaling pathways. From: A novel method of transcriptional response analysis to facilitate drug repositioning for cancer therapy.

The right side is for the signal cascade in the cell cycle G1/S checkpoint signaling pathway while the left side is for the P53 signaling pathway. The drug-dose pairs are listed in the middle. The drug-targeted pathway was generated using IPA software (Ingenuity Systems, Inc. Redwood City, CA).
