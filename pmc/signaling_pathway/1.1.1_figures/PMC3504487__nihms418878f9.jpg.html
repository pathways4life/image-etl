Fig. 9. Elevation of activity impairs Erk1/2-STAT3 signaling to control p53 expression. From: Loss of STAT3 signaling during elevated activity causes vulnerability in hippocampal neurons.

In mature neurons, Erk1/2 activates STAT3, and STAT3 binds and regulates p53 transcription, leading to resistance to the loss of neurotrophin-Akt survival signaling. This alternative survival pathway is impaired when neurons are challenged by elevated activity.
