Figure 5. From: Puerarin Suppress Apoptosis of Human Osteoblasts via ERK Signaling Pathway.

ERK signaling pathway and estrogen receptor mediated the antiapoptotic effects of puerarin in hOBs. Cells were incubated with PD98059 (10 μM) and/or ICI 182780 (10 μM) for 3 h prior to treatment with 10−8 M puerarin for 48 h. The bars represented the mean ± SD (n = 6). *P < 0.05, as compared with the puerarin-treated cells; **P < 0.05, as compared with the serum-free treated cells.
