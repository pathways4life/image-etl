Fig. 6. The RIG-I signaling pathway and downstream components of IRF3 activation are not affected by SRT and TFP. From: A high throughput screening for TLR3-IRF3 signaling pathway modulators identifies several antipsychotic drugs as TLR inhibitors.

HEK293 cells were co-transfected with ISG56-luciferase and Î²-actin Renilla luciferase, together with RIG-I (A) and IRF-3-5D mutant (B) for 24 h. Following transfection, cells were treated with SRT or TFP at the indicated concentrations for another 8 h. Cell lyasates were analyzed for firefly and Renila luciferese activities using the Dual Luciferase Reporter Assay System. The results were corrected for transfection efficiencies with Renilla luciferase and expressed as fold induction of ISG56 luciferase for RIG-I or IRF-3-5D transfected cells relative to those of vector transfected controls. The results were representative of two similar experiments.
