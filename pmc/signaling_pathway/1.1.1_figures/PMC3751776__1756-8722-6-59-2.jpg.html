Figure 2. From: Ibrutinib and novel BTK inhibitors in clinical development.

BTK signaling pathway. BTK translocates to the plasma membrane by interacting with PIP3 to become membrane-bound where it undergoes sequential activation through trans-phosphorylation by Lyn and Syk kinases, followed by autophosphorylation. The downstream substrates of activated BTK and their associated signaling cascades are indicated.
