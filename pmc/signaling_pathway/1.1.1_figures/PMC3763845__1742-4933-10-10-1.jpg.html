Figure 1. From: Pinpointing beta adrenergic receptor in ageing pathophysiology: victim or executioner? Evidence from crime scenes.

Classical pathway of β2adrenergic receptor (β2AR) activating Gαsprotein, which in turn activates the conversion of ATP in cAMP by the adenylate cyclase (AC). An alternative signaling pathway involves Gαi protein. G-protein-linked receptor kinase 2 (GRK2) and β-Arrestin participate in the desensitization process of the receptor.
