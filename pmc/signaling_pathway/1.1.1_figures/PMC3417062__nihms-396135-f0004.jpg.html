Fig. 4. From: PI3K/AKT pathway alterations are associated with clinically aggressive and histologically anaplastic subsets of pilocytic astrocytoma.

AKT pathway signaling is increased in histologically anaplastic PA (APA) when compared with conventional and clinically aggressive PA. Tissue microarray sections stained with immunohistochemistry using total protein and phospho-specific antibodies (a). Semiquantification results corresponding to the immunohistochemistry tests are illustrated on the right (b) (white bars conventional PA; gray bars clinically aggressive PA; black bars APA(B)(Wilcoxon rank sum, error bars SEM)
