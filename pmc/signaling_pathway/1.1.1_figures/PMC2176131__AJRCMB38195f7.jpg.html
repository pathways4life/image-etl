Figure 7.. From: Collagen I Promotes Epithelial-to-Mesenchymal Transition in Lung Cancer Cells via Transforming Growth Factor-? Signaling.

Model for EMT in A549 lung cancer cells. Extracellular stimuli such as collagen I or EGF activate PI3K/ERK signaling, which stimulates transcription of TGF-β3 mRNA and autocrine secretion of TGF-β3 protein, which binds to its receptor on the surface of the cell, activating the Smad pathway and up-regulating genes that promote EMT.
