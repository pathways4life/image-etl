Figure 1. Role of each metric in assessing different aspects of signaling biology.. From: Functional Characterization of FLT3 Receptor Signaling Deregulation in Acute Myeloid Leukemia by Single Cell Network Profiling (SCNP).

Summary schema of all metrics used in the two studies and the role each has in assessing different aspects of signaling and pathway biology. Median Fluorescence Intensities (MFI) were calculated for leukemic blast cells in each condition and used to compute metrics for Relative Expression of surface expression markers and Basal, Fold and Total measurements of signaling as described in Results. Definitions of metrics used to analyze surface marker expression, signaling data, and apoptosis readouts are shown (i.e. Basal  =  log2(UnmodulatedMFI/AutofluorescenceMFI).
