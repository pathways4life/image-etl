Figure 9. A putative model for the molecular pathway linking hypoxic stress to decreased AQP5 expression.. From: Hypoxia and Hypoxia Mimetics Decrease Aquaporin 5 (AQP5) Expression through Both Hypoxia Inducible Factor-1? and Proteasome-Mediated Pathways.

Hypoxic stress activates HIF-1α via the ERK signaling pathway. HIF-1α activates a repressor of AQP5 transcription via a proteasome-mediated mechanism (which could act by degrading inhibitors of the repressor protein).
