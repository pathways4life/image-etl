Figure 1 . From: Specific targeting of IL-6 signalling pathway: a new way to treat IBD?.

The receptor for IL-6 consists of the ligand binding component (IL-6R) and the signal-transducing gp130. The soluble form of the IL-6R may form a complex with IL-6 having the ability to bind gp130 (trans signaling) expressed by T cells and to activate the anti-apoptotic STAT-3 dependent genes bcl-2 and bcl-xl. In inflammatory diseases, targeting of IL-6 signaling pathway modulates inflammation at least in part through the regulation of T cell expansion and the expression of adhesion molecules.
