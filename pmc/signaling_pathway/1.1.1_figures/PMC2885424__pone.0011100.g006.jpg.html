Figure 6. Heat map summary and hierarchical clustering of gene expression.. From: Transcriptional Regulation of the IGF Signaling Pathway by Amino Acids and Insulin-Like Growth Factors during Myogenesis in Atlantic Salmon.

Heat map summary and hierarchical clustering for MRFs, MEF2A, pax7, FGF2 and components of the IGF signaling pathway during Atlantic salmon myogenic cell culture at 2 d, 5 d, 8 d, 11 d, 14 d, 17 d and 20 d. Rows are standardized to have mean 0 and standard deviation 1 so that red indicates high and green indicates low values.
