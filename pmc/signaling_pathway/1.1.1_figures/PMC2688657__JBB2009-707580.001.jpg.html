Figure 1. From: Integrating Multiple Microarray Data for Cancer Pathway Analysis Using Bootstrapping K-S Test.

Antigrowth signaling pathway and cluster around SMAD proteins. (a) Antigrowth signaling pathway. Nodes and edges represent human proteins and protein-protein interactions, respectively. Edges with direction represent a regulatory relation. → means an activating relation and, ⊣ means an inhibitory relation. (b) Cluster around smads. Red edges represent differentially changed relations. Blue edges represent unchanged relations. Red nodes represent tumor suppressor genes, and green nodes represent oncogenes.
