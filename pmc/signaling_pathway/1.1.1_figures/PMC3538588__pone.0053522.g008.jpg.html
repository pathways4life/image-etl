Figure 8. Genome to phenome pathway of TGF-beta signaling.. From: Systems Biology Approach Reveals Genome to Phenome Correlation in Type 2 Diabetes.

T2D genome, T2D interactome, T2D transcriptome and antidiabetic drug interacting genes are mapped on to KEGG pathway for TGF-beta signaling. Red: genome, interactome and transcriptome; brown: interactome, transcriptome and antidiabetic drug interacting genes; green: interactome and transcriptome; yellow: interactome; grey: antidiabetic drug interacting genes.
