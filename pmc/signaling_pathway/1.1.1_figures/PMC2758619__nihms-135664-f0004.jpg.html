Figure 4. Signaling pathway activation in mammary tumors. From: NEDD9 promotes oncogenic signaling in mammary tumor development.

A. Signaling interactions of Nedd9 relevant to mammary tumor development. B, C. Western analysis demonstrates expression and activation levels of the indicated proteins in primary tumor lysates, and quantification of relative difference in activation between Nedd9-/- and Nedd9+/+ mice. D. Degree of pathway activation as a correlate of initial tumor appearance (see data in Figures 1A, 1C). The statistical trend is highly significant for time of initial detection of tumors (p<0.001 for all proteins evaluated).
