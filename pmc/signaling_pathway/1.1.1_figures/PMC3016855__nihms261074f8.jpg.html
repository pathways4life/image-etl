FIGURE 8. From: Calcium-Activated Pathways and Oxidative Burst Mediate Zymosan-Induced Signaling and IL-10 Production in Human Macrophages.

In human primary macrophages, zymosan activates a calcium-dependent CaMKII–Pyk2–ERK signaling pathway downstream of Dectin-1 and ITAM-associated kinases Src and Syk. Calcium pathways also induce CREB phosphorylation and generation of ROS. ERK, CREB, and ROS contribute to calcium-mediated IL-10 production.
