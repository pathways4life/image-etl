Figure 4. The insulin signaling pathway is predicted to be targeted by many aging-regulated novel miRNA candidates.. From: Novel MicroRNAs Differentially Expressed during Aging in the Mouse Brain.

Multiple novel miRNA candidates are predicted to target each of the genes implicated in the pathway, and each novel miRNA candidate is predicted to target multiple genes in the pathway. In red: upregulated (>2.0-fold) novel miRNAs; in black: downregulated (>2.0-fold) novel miRNAs.
