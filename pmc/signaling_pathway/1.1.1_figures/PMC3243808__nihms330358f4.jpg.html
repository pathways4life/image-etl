Figure 4. IL-6 mediates EMT changes via the JAK/STAT3 signaling pathway. From: IL-6 promotes head and neck tumor metastasis by inducing epithelial-mesenchymal transition via the JAK-STAT3-SNAIL signaling pathway.

Akt, STAT3, or ERK1/2 proteins were knocked down in CAL-IL6 and IOE-IL-6 cells by respective siRNAs. A-B: Expression level of different proteins (Akt, STAT3, ERK1/2, E-cadherin, vimentin, and snail) was examined in CAL-IL-6 (A) or IOE-IL-6 (B) cells by Western blotting. Equal protein loading was verified by stripping the blots and reprobing with tubulin antibody. Band density of each protein was normalized with tubulin and expressed as % increase or decrease ± SE as compared to controls. C-D: Expression levels of E-cadherin (red) and vimentin (green) was examined in CAL-IL-6 cells (C) or IOE-IL-6 cells (D) by fluorescent microscope (600×).
