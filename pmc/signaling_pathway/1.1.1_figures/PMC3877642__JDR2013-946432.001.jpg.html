Figure 1. From: Effects of Acute Exercise and Chronic Exercise on the Liver Leptin-AMPK-ACC Signaling Pathway in Rats with Type 2 Diabetes.

Histograph showing the effects of exercise on the leptin-AMPK-ACC pathway in type 2 diabetes rats. The relative levels of protein phosphorylation and protein expression related to the leptin-AMPK-ACC signaling pathway on different groups were shown. (a) Leptin and its receptor. (b) AMPK1/2 and p-AMPK1/2 (Thr172). (c) AMPKα1 and p-AMPKα1 (Thr172). (d) AMPKα2 and p-AMPKα2 (Thr172). (e) ACC and p-ACC (Ser79). Note: in comparison with the control group: **P < 0.01, *P < 0.05; in comparison with the diabetes group: ##P < 0.01, #P < 0.05 (n = 8).
