FIGURE 10. From: Peptidoglycan Induces Cyclooxygenase-2 Expression in Macrophages by Activating the Neutral Sphingomyelinase-Ceramide Pathway.

Schematic diagram illustrating the proposed signaling pathway involved in PGN-induced COX-2 expression in macrophages through two separate pathways: the Ras/Raf-1/ERK pathway to induce IKKα/β activation and the nSMase/ceramide pathway to induce MKK3/6/p38 MAPK activation cascades resulting in an increase in NF-κB transactivation, ultimately causing COX-2 expression in macrophages.
