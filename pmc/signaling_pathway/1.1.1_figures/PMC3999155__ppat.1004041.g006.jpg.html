Figure 6. Working model of TRAF3-dependent regulation of RLR signaling pathway by HSCARG.. From: HSCARG Negatively Regulates the Cellular Antiviral RIG-I Like Receptor Signaling Pathway by Inhibiting TRAF3 Ubiquitination via Recruiting OTUB1.

Followed viral infection, HSCARG interacts with TRAF3, and cooperates with OTUB1 to remove Lys63-linked polyubiquitin chain from TRAF3. This further decreases the recruitment of IKKε and results in decreased phosphorylation of IKKε and IRF3, and finally reduces the production of IFN-β.
