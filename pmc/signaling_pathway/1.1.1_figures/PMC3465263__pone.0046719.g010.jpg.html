Figure 10. Possible points of interaction between Pcmt1 and the upstream insulin-signaling pathway.. From: Wortmannin Reduces Insulin Signaling and Death in Seizure-Prone Pcmt1−/− Mice.

Wortmannin inhibits PI3K class kinases and has been shown to inhibit insulin signaling. The elevated insulin signaling in Pcmt1−/− animals could arise from aberrant Akt activation (hypothesis 1 or 2), and/or aberrant interaction between Akt1 and mTOR (hypothesis 1 or 2) or activation of an alternate growth pathway (hypothesis 3).
