Fig. 6. Schematic representation of the network of metabolic and signaling pathways in proliferating cells treated with CP-320626. From: Inhibition of glycogen phosphorylation induces changes in cellular proteome and signaling pathways in MIA pancreatic cancer cells.

This schema shows the interconnected network of the key metabolic and signaling pathways, including glycolysis, oxidative phosphorylation, the pentose phosphate pathway, and critical cell signaling pathways. Biological macromolecular synthesis can be influenced by these key metabolic and signaling pathways. The critical metabolic and signaling pathways are labeled in blue with boxes. The up-regulated proteins are shown in red and down-regulated proteins are in green
