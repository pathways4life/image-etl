Figure 4. The effects of PTE on proteins in the Notch1 signaling pathway and the mitochondrial apoptotic pathway in lung adenocarcinoma cells treated for 24 h.. From: Pterostilbene Exerts Antitumor Activity via the Notch1 Signaling Pathway in Human Lung Adenocarcinoma Cells.

(A) Representative Western blot results are shown. The PTE treatment increased the expression levels of NICD, Hes1, Nicastrin, Presenilin-1, Bax and Cytochrome c. (B) Representative images of Notch1 immunofluorescence are shown (×400). (C) Representative images of Hes1 immunofluorescence are shown (×400). The results are expressed as the mean ± SEM, n = 6. **P<0.01 compared with the control group, ##P<0.01 compared with the PTE 1.5 µM group, $$P<0.01 compared with the PTE 3 µM group. PTE, pterostilbene.
