Figure 3. From: hiPathDB: a human-integrated pathway database with facile visualization.

Reformatted modeling of the BioCarta ‘apoptotic signaling in response to DNA damage’ pathway. Note that + and−next to the gene circles indicate the active and inactive forms, respectively. Small red ovals indicate translocation, and the new location of protein is provided next to the oval (yellow box, biological processes; blue circle, gene or protein; gray circle, pseudo node; red circle, complex node).
