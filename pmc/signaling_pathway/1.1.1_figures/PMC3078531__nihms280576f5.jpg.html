Fig. 5. Proposed ROS-dependent signaling pathways for GATA4 activation. From: Cell signalling pathways for the regulation of GATA4 transcription factor: Implications for cell growth and apoptosis.

Signaling stimuli trigger the generation of ROS, which in turn (i) activate MEK, phosphorylating ERK and (ii) activate RhoA/ROCK pathway, facilitating ERK nuclear translocation. In the nucleus, ERK then phosphorylates and activates GATA4.
