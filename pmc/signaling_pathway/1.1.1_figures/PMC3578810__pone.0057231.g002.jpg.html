Figure 2. Apelin improves insulin signaling pathway in the hepatocytes treated by TNF-α.. From: Apelin Ameliorates TNF-?-Induced Reduction of Glycogen Synthesis in the Hepatocytes through G Protein-Coupled Receptor APJ.

Apelin-13 improved insulin signaling pathway (JNK-IRS1-AKT-GSK) in HepG2 cells (A) and mouse primary hepatocytes (B) treated with 10 ng/ml TNF-α for 24 h. Data represent the means ± S.E.M., n = 3 independent experiments. * p<0.05; ** p<0.01 and *** p<0.001 by ANOVA test (v.s. control or TNF-α).
