Figure 5. Analysis of adipogenic-specific signaling pathways.. From: Reverse Differentiation as a Gene Filtering Tool in Genome Expression Profiling of Adipogenesis for Fat Marker Gene Selection and Their Analysis.

The 991 genes that were differentially expressed during adipogenesis were uploaded to the KEGG database to determine their involvement in adipogenic signaling pathways. We found different signaling cascades for adipogenesis like the (A) insulin signaling pathway, (B) PPARG signaling pathway, (C) fatty acid biosynthesis pathway, (D) adipocytokine signaling pathway, (E) fatty acid elongation pathway, (F) pathway for biosynthesis of unsaturated fatty acids and (G) pathway for fatty acid metabolism.
