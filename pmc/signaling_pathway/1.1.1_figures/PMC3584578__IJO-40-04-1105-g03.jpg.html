Figure 4. From: MicroRNA miR-451 downregulates the PI3K/AKT pathway through CAB39 in human glioma.

Impact of the miR-451 mimic oligonucleotide on some of the components of the PI3K/AKT signaling pathway. (A) U251, LN229, A172 and U87 cells were treated with the miR-451 mimics and LKB1, AMPK, p-AMPK, PI3K, p-AKT and GAPDH expressions were determined by Western blotting 48 h after treatment. (B) Schematic diagram of how miR-451 downregulation could lead to activation of the PI3K/AKT pathway.
