Figure 3. Intramolecular signaling pathway for PSD-95 with truncated third alpha helix predicted by the PRS method.. From: Change in Allosteric Network Affects Binding Affinities of PDZ Domains: Analysis through Perturbation Response Scanning.

We obtain the allosteric pathway for the truncated PSD-95 through connections Ile314 → Ile 327 → Glu334 → His372 → Ile380 → Ile388. However, the predicted allosteric pathway of full PSD-95 is different. The interactions specifically located in the α1 helix predicted for the PSD-95 were lost after removal of the α3 helix.
