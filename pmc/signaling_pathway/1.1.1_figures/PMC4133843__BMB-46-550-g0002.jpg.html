Fig. 2.miR-15b is regulated by the PDGF signaling pathway. (A) Time-course expression of miR-15b and miR-221 normalized to U6 small nuclear RNA was examined by qRT-PCR in PASMC treated with PDGF-BB (20 ng/ml) for the time indicated.
