Figure 14. From: Integrating In Silico Resources to Map a Signaling Network.

Pathway databases. (A) EGFR pathway map from BioCarta (http://www.biocarta.com/pathfiles/h_egfPathway.asp; permission to use this image was granted by BioCarta, LLC). (B) Overlap of pathway entities for the EGFR signaling pathway among pathway datasets retrieved from Netpath, BioCarta, Protein Lounge, STKE and Cell Designer (CellDes). 371 unique interactors were collectively identified using all five pathway databases. Out of these 371 interactors, only 103, 46, 32, 15 or 10 interactors were mentioned in at least two, three, four or all five databases, respectively. For investigators who are interested in only ‘High-Confidence’ datasets (Figure 10), the 10 interactors identified in all five databases mentioned in this example, would be of particular interest.
