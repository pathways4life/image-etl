Fig. 1. Upd activated long-range JAK/STAT signaling. From: Drosophila glypicans Dally and Dally-like are essential regulators for JAK/STAT signaling and Unpaired distribution in eye development.

A-B: The JAK/STAT signaling pathway was activated in eye discs in 2nd instar larval stage (A-A”) and early 3rd instar larval stage (B-B”), which was shown by 10XSTAT92E-GFP (green) and pSTAT staining (red). C-D: JAK/STAT signal can be activated in the cells distant to cells expressing upd in both 2nd instar larval stage (C-C”) and early 3rd instar larval stage (D-D”). Upd-lacZ expression at the posterior center (red in C’ and D’) marks the cells secreting Upd. E-F: Upd was distributed far from secreting cells in both 2nd instar larval stage (E-E”) and 3rd instar larval stage (F-F”). Extracellular Upd staining (green in E’ and F’) can be detected in the entire eye disc. In all images, the anterior compartment faces left, and the dorsal compartment faces up.
