Figure 2. From: Extracellular regulation of BMP signaling.

Self-regulation in Xenopus dorsal-ventral patterning.The pathway consists of a network of BMP-interacting proteins. The diagram indicates extracellular biochemical protein–protein interactions as black arrows, transcriptional regulation under the control of BMP signaling as green arrows, and the directional flow of Chordin–BMP complexes from the dorsal to the ventral side of the embryo as red arrows.
