Figure 2. From: Growth arrest signaling of the Raf/MEK/ERK pathway in cancer.

Two different thresholds of Raf/MEK/ERK activity determine cell fate to proliferation or growth arrest. In this model, not only too low but also too high Raf/MEK/ERK activity restricts cell proliferation. Different cell types may maintain different extent of pathway activity between these thresholds, displaying heterogeneous sensitivity to pathway activity.
