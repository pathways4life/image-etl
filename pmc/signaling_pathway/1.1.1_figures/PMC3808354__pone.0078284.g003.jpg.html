Figure 3. Uric acid causes excessive nitric oxide production in pancreatic β-cells via the NF-κB signaling pathway.. From: Hyperuricemia Causes Pancreatic ?-Cell Death and Dysfunction through NF-?B Signaling Pathway.

(A) The NO content, determined by a Griess assay kit, of Min6 cells treated with different concentrations of uric acid. NO production increased in a concentration-dependent manner. (B) Uric acid treatment of Min6 cells induced an increase in iNOS mRNA levels, as detected by RT-PCR. This effect was reversed by 50 µmol/L benzbromarone. (C) 5 µmol/L BAY 11–7082 also reversed the uric acid-induced increase in iNOS mRNA levels. **P<0.01.
