Fig. 8. From: Myb promotes centriole amplification and later steps of the multiciliogenesis program.

Proposed molecular pathway governing multiciliogenesis. MYB and other transcriptional regulators associated with each step in the program are shown along with the regulatory relationships among them, together with the Notch signaling pathway that controls initiation of the program. The dashed line indicates that there is an auxiliary pathway that can partially bypass the requirement for MYB, at least in some MCCs.
