Figure 5. JNK signaling was involved in CD9-regulated MMP-9 production.. From: Downregulation of CD9 in Keratinocyte Contributes to Cell Migration via Upregulation of Matrix Metalloproteinase-9 .

(A) Representative western blot results showing increased JNK phosphorylation in CD9-shRNA adenovirus-infected HaCaT cells, with no alteration to p38 pathway and ERK pathway. (B) Representative western blot results and data summary of p-JNK and JNK in HaCaT cells infected with CD9-shRNA and Ad-CD9 adenovirus (n = 5). (C) Gelatin zymograms were shown for activity of MMP-9 and MMP-2 in the culture supernatants (n = 3). Mock-infected and CD9-silencing HaCaT cells were treated with JNK signaling inhibitor, SP600125 (SP). (D) Western blots were shown for MMP-9 and MMP-2 (n = 3). *P< 0.05 vs. Mock group. #P< 0.05 vs. Vector group. **P< 0.05 vs. CD9-shRNA group.
