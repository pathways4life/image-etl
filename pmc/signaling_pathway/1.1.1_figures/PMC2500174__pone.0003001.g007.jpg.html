Figure 7. A model summarizing the Rho-Rock-Myosin signaling pathway that regulates basic cell-cell interactions in ES cells.. From: The Rho-Rock-Myosin Signaling Axis Determines Cell-Cell Integrity of Self-Renewing Pluripotent Stem Cells.

Chemicals and siRNAs used in the study are highlighted in red and asterisks, respectively. Dotted lines indicate potential mechanistic interactions within or between the cell-integrity and self-renewal pathways, although further investigations are required to address these possibilities. Arrows denote activation and bars indicate inhibition.
