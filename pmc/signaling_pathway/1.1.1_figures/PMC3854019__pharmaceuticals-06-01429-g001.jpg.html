Figure 1. From: Hedgehog Pathway Blockade Inhibits Melanoma Cell Growth in Vitro and in Vivo.

Hedgehog pathway members are upregulated in a subset of melanoma cell lines as measured via affymetrix microarray analysis. SMO, GLI2, PTCH1, and WNT5a are upregulated, while negative regulators of hedgehog signaling such as protein kinase A (PRKA) and dual specificity tyrosine-phosphorylation-regulated kinase 2 (DYRK2) are downregulated as compared to melanocytes.
