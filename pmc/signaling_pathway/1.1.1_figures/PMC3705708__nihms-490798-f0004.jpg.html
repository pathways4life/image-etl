Fig. 4. The Hh signaling network. From: The Hedgehog Signal Transduction Network.

In vertebrates, Hh binding to Ptc modulates one or more of the following signaling modules: non-canonical type I Hh signaling exclusively through Ptc; canonical Hh signaling through Ptc, Smo, and Glis (mediated in some instances by Gi proteins); and a noncanonical type II signaling through Smo and Gi proteins. The cellular responses to each signaling module are illustrated at the bottom. The red crosses indicate inhibition of the pathway in the presence of Hh proteins.
