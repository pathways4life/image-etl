Schematic representation of TNF/Fas/TRAIL signaling pathway. By analyzing our proteomics data, antibody microarray data, and our previously published binding protein data (20), a number of signaling pathways were identified, and the TNF/Fas/TRAIL signaling pathway is highlighted here.
