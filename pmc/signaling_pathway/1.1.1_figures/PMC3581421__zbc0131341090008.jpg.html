FIGURE 8. From: Sphingosine 1-Phosphate (S1P) Receptors 1 and 2 Coordinately Induce Mesenchymal Cell Migration through S1P Activation of Complementary Kinase Pathways.

Schematic of coupling and S1P signaling in mesenchymal cells. Osteoclast SPHK generates S1P, which activates receptors S1PR1 and S1PR2 on mesenchymal cells. S1PR1 activated the JAK/STAT pathway, and S1PR2 activates the FAK/PI3K/AKT pathway to stimulate MSC migration.
