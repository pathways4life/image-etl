Fig. 1. From: Inhibition of CK2 binding to BMPRIa induces C2C12 differentiation into osteoblasts and adipocytes.

BMP2 signaling pathway resulting in osteogenesis or adipogenesis. a Ligand binding to BMPRIa and BMPRII results in the release of CK2, followed by phosphorylation of BMPRIa by a constitutively active BMPRII, leading to signaling. b Signaling via Smad1, 5, and 8, ERK, PI3K, JNK, p38, and NF-kB results in osteogenesis. c Activation of Smad1, 5, and 8 and p38 pathways are implicated in adipogenesis
