Figure 7. Pathway analysis and gene expression subtype analysis.. From: A Fourteen Gene GBM Prognostic Signature Identifies Association of Immune Response Pathway and Mesenchymal Subtype with High Risk Group.

A) KEGG pathway enrichment analysis. Enrichment of cytokine-cytokine receptor interaction and chemokine signaling pathways leads to activation of various pro-survival pathways like Jak-STAT pathway, MAPK signaling pathway and NFkB pathway with the resultant pro-tumorigenic environment. B) TCGA patients (n = 108) were divided into gene expression sub types – proneural, neural, mesenchymal and classical among low risk and high risk groups. Mann Whitney test was carried out to find out significance of distribution of a given expression subtype within a risk group.
