Fig. 7. From: Complex Human Glucocorticoid Receptor dim Mutations Define Glucocorticoid Induced Apoptotic Resistance in Bone Cells.

Regulation of genes in the apoptotic signaling pathway. A, Using Ingenuity Pathways Analysis, genes involved in apoptosis signaling were shown to be regulated by hGRα+DEX, including Bcl-xL (down-regulated) and CASP6 (up-regulated) (n = 3; *, P < 0.01). B, Ingenuity Pathways Analysis of genes involved in apoptosis signaling and regulated by hGRdim4+DEX. These included cIAP2 (up-regulated) and Gas2 (up-regulated) (n = 3; *, P < 0.01). C, Total RNA was isolated from hGRα, hGRdim, and hGRdim4 cells treated with 100 nm DEX for 6 h and gene regulation analyzed using quantitative RT-PCR. Four genes involved in apoptosis signaling were shown to be differentially regulated by hGRα (Bcl-xL and CASP6) or hGRdim and hGRdim4 (cIAP2 and Gas2) (n = 3; *, P < 0.01). CON, Control.
