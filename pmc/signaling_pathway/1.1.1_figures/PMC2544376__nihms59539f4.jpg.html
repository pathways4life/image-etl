Figure 4. TGF-β and Ras signaling associated with vimentin expression. From: Intestinal Adenomagenesis Involves Core Molecular Signatures of the Epithelial-Mesenchymal Transition.

A. No vimentin signal detected by IHC in a Smad-3-deficient 129 Min adenoma. B. Vimentin (brown) detected by IHC in a Smad-3 wildtype 129 Min adenoma. Comparison of the Ras signaling pathway indicated by (C) IHC for pMAPK and (D) vimentin expression by ISH in a B6 Min adenoma. Black arrows, regions with strong pMAPK staining and weak vimentin expression. White-filled arrows, regions with weak pMAPK staining and strong vimentin expression. Size bars, 200 µm.
