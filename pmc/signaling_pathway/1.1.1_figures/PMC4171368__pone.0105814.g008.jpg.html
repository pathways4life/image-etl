Figure 8. The proposed anti-apoptotic signaling pathway induced by DP in normal and AR neutrophils.. From: Regulation of Constitutive Neutrophil Apoptosis Due to House Dust Mite Allergen in Normal and Allergic Rhinitis Subjects.

Anti-apoptotic signaling due to DP involves the activation of PKCδ, ERK and NF-κB and is associated with suppression of procaspase 3 and procaspase 9 cleavage.
