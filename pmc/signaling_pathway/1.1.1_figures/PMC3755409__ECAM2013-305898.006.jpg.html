Figure 6. From: Therapeutic Potential of Andrographolide Isolated from the Leaves of Andrographis paniculata Nees for Treating Lung Adenocarcinomas.

Scheme of the andrographolide regulatory pathway. The effects of andrographolide inhibition of lung tumors are hypothesized through a cell-cycle signaling pathway. The diagram shows that andrographolide may inhibit VEGF-A, KDR, Nrp1, ERK2, Cyclin A, and Cyclin B expression.
