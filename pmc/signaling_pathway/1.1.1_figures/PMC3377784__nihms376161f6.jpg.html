Fig. 6. Potential mechanisms for SOD2-mediated TSCC metastasis in association with Snail signaling. From: Manganese superoxide dismutase induces migration and invasion of tongue squamous cell carcinoma via H2O2-dependent Snail signaling.

Sod2 is activated by a variety of stimuli leading to an increase in the steady-state production of H2O2. H2O2 activates the Snail-ERK1/2-MMP1 signaling pathway resulting in TSCC metastasis.
