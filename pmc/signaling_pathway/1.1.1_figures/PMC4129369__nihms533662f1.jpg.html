Figure 1. Immunohistochemical detection of TGF-β and BMP pathway signaling in pediatric germ cell tumors. From: BMP Signaling Activity Distinguishes Histologic Subsets of Pediatric Germ Cell Tumors.

Representative images from a tissue microarray containing 14 yolk sac tumors (A–F) and 10 germinomatous tumors (seminomas, dysgerminomas or CNS germinomas) (G–L). C, D, I, J: Phosphorylated-SMAD3 immunohistochemistry as a readout of TGF-β signaling activity. E, F, K, L: Phosphorylated-SMAD1/5/8 immunohistochemistry as a readout of BMP signaling activity. Scoring results for individual tumors are summarized in Table 1. Scale bar: 100 microns.
