Signaling pathway whereby tumor associated macrophages promote Wnt signaling in tumor cells. Peripheral blood monocytes (Mo) were cultured with control medium or with conditioned medium from HCT116 or Hke-3 cells for 48 h. As shown here, soluble factor(s) from HCT116 and Hke-3 cells induced maturation of normal peripheral blood monocytes (Mo), demonstrated by phalloidin/DAPI staining, coupled to the release of IL-1β.
