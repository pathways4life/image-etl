Figure 6. Schematic model of TROY-Pyk2-Rac1 signaling pathway in GBM cells. From: TROY (TNFRSF19) is Overexpressed in Advanced Glial Tumors and Promotes Glioblastoma Cell Invasion via Pyk2-Rac1 Signaling.

Activation of the TROY receptor by possible interaction with NgR1-LINGO complex elicits a signaling pathway to recruit Pyk2 to the TROY receptor complex. Pyk2 functions to induce Rac1 activation potentially by influencing a Rac guanine nucleotide exchange factor (GEF) to drive glioma cell migration and invasion.
