Figure 3. Signaling pathways triggered by GDNF in spermatogonial stem cells. From: Human spermatogonial stem cells: a possible origin for spermatocytic seminoma.

GDNF dimerizes and binds to the GFRÎ±-1/RET receptor complex. A: Binding of GDNF activates RET, which triggers SRC kinase phosphorylation and the downstream activation of PI3K/AKT. Ultimately the transcription factor MYCN is up-regulated. B: Binding of GDNF also can activate the RAS-mediated signaling pathway, which triggers ERK1/2 phosphorylation and up-regulation of the transcription factor FOS.
