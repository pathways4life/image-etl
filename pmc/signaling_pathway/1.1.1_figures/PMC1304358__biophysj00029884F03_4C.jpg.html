FIGURE 3. From: The JAK-STAT Signaling Network in the Human B-Cell: An Extreme Signaling Pathway Analysis.

Examples of extreme signaling pathways of the JAK-STAT signaling network. Pathway 119 (A) corresponds to the binding of the interferon γ ligand to its receptor and the subsequent formation of an activated receptor-ligand complex. Pathway 1 (B) corresponds to the activation of the STAT1-STAT2 heterodimer by the interferon γ and interferon α/β bound receptors. The names of the reactions are in italics above the reaction arrows (see supplementary material).
