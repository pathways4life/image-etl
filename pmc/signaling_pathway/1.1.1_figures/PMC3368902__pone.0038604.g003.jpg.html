Figure 3. Temporal profile of components of the insulin signaling pathway in rainbow trout, as determined by Western Blot densitometry.. From: Postprandial Regulation of Hepatic MicroRNAs Predicted to Target the Insulin Pathway in Rainbow Trout.

Means and S.E. of n = 6 samples per group are shown. Data were analysed using a one-way ANOVA, followed by the Newman-Keuls post-hoc test. Different letters indicate a significant difference at p<0.05.
