Figure 8. A schematic model of ZnR signaling in the CA3 region of the hippocampus. From: Synaptically-Released Zinc Triggers Metabotropic Signaling via a Zinc Sensing Receptor in the Hippocampus.

Synaptic Zn2+, released from the mossy fibers, activates a metabotropic ZnR. The ZnR activity induces metabotropic release of Ca2+ mediated by a GÎ±q protein, GPR39, and the IP3 pathway, leading to phosphorylation of extracellular-regulated kinase (ERK1/2) and Ca2+/calmodulin kinase (CAMKII).
