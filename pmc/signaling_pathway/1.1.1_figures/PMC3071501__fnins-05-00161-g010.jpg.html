Figure 10. From: Transcriptomic Analysis in a Drosophila Model Identifies Previously Implicated and Novel Pathways in the Therapeutic Mechanism in Neuropsychiatric Disorders.

Genes mapping to KEGG pathway for hedgehog signaling. Pink boxes represent upregulated genes in AED untreated flies. These genes are Rab23 (CG2108 gene product from transcript CG2108-RA), dpp (decapentaplegic), and fu (fused). Green boxes represent other members in the pathway database for Drosophila melanogaster.
