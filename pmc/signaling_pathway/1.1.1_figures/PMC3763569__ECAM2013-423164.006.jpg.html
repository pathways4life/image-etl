Figure 6. From: Berberine Reduces the Metastasis of Chondrosarcoma by Modulating the αvβ3 Integrin and the PKCδ, c-Src, and AP-1 Signaling Pathways.

Schematic presentation of the signaling pathways involved in berberine-inhibited metastasis of human chondrosarcoma. Berberine inhibits the migration and invasion of human chondrosarcoma cells by modulating the αvβ3 integrin through PKCδ, c-Src, and AP-1 signaling pathway.
