FIG. 2. From: Drosophila Insulin Pathway Mutants Affect Visual Physiology and Brain Function Besides Growth, Lipid, and Carbohydrate Metabolism.

Insulin signaling is required for lipid and carbohydrate metabolism. A: Histogram shows the percentage increases in lipids of heteroallelic and homozygous mutant flies for the insulin pathway compared with paired heterozygous sibling controls. B: Histogram shows the percentage change in body carbohydrates of heteroallelic and homozygous mutant flies for the insulin pathway compared with paired heterozygous siblings (n = 8). *P < 0.01. The error bars represent the SEM.
