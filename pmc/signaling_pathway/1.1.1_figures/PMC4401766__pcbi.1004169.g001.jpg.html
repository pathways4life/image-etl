Fig 1. Insulin signaling in glioma.. From: Simulation Predicts IGFBP2-HIF1α Interaction Drives Glioblastoma Growth.

(A) Microscope image of glioma spheroids grown in vitro. Inset illustrates detailed intracellular insulin signaling. HIF1α = hypoxia-inducible factor 1 α, IGFBP2 = Insulin like growth factor binding protein 2, IGFI = Insulin like growth factor 1, IGFIR = Insulin like growth factor receptor 1, VHL = von Hippel-Lindau complex, PHDs = prolyl hydroxylase domain proteins and Ub = Ubiquitinated. (B) Schematic of the simplified insulin signaling pathway used in the computational model.
