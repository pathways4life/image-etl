Figure 3. Signaling proteins present in LPS-treated thioglycollate-elicited macrophages derived from C57BL/6 WT and LMP7−/−/MECL-1−/− double knockout mice. From: The Immunoproteasomes Regulate Lps-Induced Trif/Tram Signaling Pathway in Murine Macrophages.

Macrophages were cultured in vitro and treated with LPS (200 ng/ml) for 0, 0.5, 1, 2, 4 and 8 h. Cell lysates were prepared and the cytoplasmic signaling proteins were analyzed by Western Blotting. This experiment was repeated three times.
