False positive distribution for PSP (A) and PDS (B). Co-localisation and expression profile of PDSs and complexes (DNA synthesome and TNF-alpha/NF-kappa B signaling complex 5) on the purine metabolism pathway. Enveloped in a checked circle are two complex outliers that are low expressing in the mod stage relative to the co-locating PDS but swung to overexpression in the poor stage
