Figure 3. Effect of leptin receptor signaling pathway on macrophage content of epididymal fat tissue (immunohistochemical staining with macrophage specific Mac-3 antibody). From: Leptin Receptor-induced STAT3-Independent Signaling Pathways are Protective against Atherosclerosis in a Murine Model of Obesity and Hyperlipidemia.

A) Lepr+/+,ApoE−/−. B) Leprs/s,ApoE−/−. C) Leprdb/db,ApoE−/−. D) % Mac-3 stained cells. E) Plasma Mcp-1 level. F) Plasma FABP4 level. N=6–8 per group. *P<0.05. Arrows point to Mac-3 positive stained cells (brown). Magnification is 40×, arrows showing stained cells.
