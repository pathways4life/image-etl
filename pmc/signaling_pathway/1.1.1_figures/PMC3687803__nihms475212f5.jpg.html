Fig. 5. From: Vitamin D and the skin: Physiology and pathophysiology.

The Sonic hedgehog (Shh) signaling pathway. In the absence of Shh, Ptch 1 suppresses signaling by smoothened (Smoh). Binding of Shh to Ptch 1 relieves this inhibition. Activation of Smoh leads to the activation and translocation of transcription factors of the Gli family into the nucleus, with subsequent changes in gene expression
