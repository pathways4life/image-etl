The Background. From: Wnt signaling and the developmental fate of lung cells.

Tadashi Okubo and Brigid Hogan [1] report in this issue of Journal of Biology a surprising result from their studies of the Wnt signaling pathway, a central cell-cell signaling pathway during development (see the 'Background' box). If a key component of this pathway is expressed in active form in the lungs of developing transgenic mouse embryos, cells appear within the lung that are more like cells of the gut than they are like their lung neighbors.
