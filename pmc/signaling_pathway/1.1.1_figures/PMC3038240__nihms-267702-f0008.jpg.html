Fig 8. Diverse inputs and outputs of Jnk signaling. From: Regulation of Hippo signaling by Jun kinase signaling during compensatory cell proliferation and regeneration, and in neoplastic tumors.

Jnk, a stress activated protein kinase, is activated by diverse stimuli, including caspase activation, ligands for Jnk signaling pathways (Egr), cellular stresses (reactive oxygen species, infection, cytoskeletal changes), and loss of apical-basal polarity (Igaki, 2009; Karin and Gallagher, 2005). Jnk activates apoptosis, but also cell proliferation. Activation of proliferation is mediated through Hippo signaling. The ultimate target of Jnk signaling within the Hippo pathway is not known, it could act upstream of Wts and Hpo, but might act in parallel to them (dashed lines and question mark). Yki both promotes cell proliferation and inhibits apoptosis through transcriptional regulation of downstream target genes.
