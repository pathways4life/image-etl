Figure 8. From: In vitro and in vivo study of effects of fermented soybean product (chungkookjang) on NGF secretion ability and NGF receptor signaling pathway.

Effects of SH-CKJ treatment on down-stream NGF receptor p75NTR signaling pathway via Western blot analysis. Total homogenates were prepared from brain tissue of Tg2576 mice treated with SH-CKJ for 8 weeks. Thirty micrograms of protein per sample was immunoblotted with antibody for each protein (A). The intensity of each band was determined using an imaging densitometer, and the relative level of each protein was calculated based on the intensity of actin (B).
