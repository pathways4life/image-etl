FIGURE 8. From: Involvement of Protein Kinase C?/Extracellular Signal-regulated Kinase/Poly(ADP-ribose) Polymerase-1 (PARP-1) Signaling Pathway in Histamine-induced Up-regulation of Histamine H1 Receptor Gene Expression in HeLa Cells.

Schematic presentation of the signaling pathway involved in histamine-induced up-regulation of H1R mRNA expression in HeLa cells. Binding of histamine to H1R activates PKCδ and translocates PKCδ from the cytosol to the Golgi where PKCδ phosphorylates MEK and ERK. Phosphorylated ERK then activates PARP-1. Poly(ADP)-ribosylation of an unknown protein (or PARP-1 itself) by PARP-1 causes activation of a transcription factor, resulting in up-regulation of H1R gene transcription.
