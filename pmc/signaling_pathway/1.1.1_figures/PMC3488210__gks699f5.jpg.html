Figure 5. From: Discovering the hidden sub-network component in a ranked list of genes or proteins derived from genomic experiments.

Significant sub-networks (see also Table 1) found among the lists of most essential genes, ranked by the RIGER parameter, obtained for both cell lines: (A) Jurkat and (B) K562. Genes are colored according to their main functions (KEGG terms): pale blue: connecting protein; blue: MAPK signaling pathway; green: ribosome; yellow: proteasome; pink: cell cycle; magenta: apoptosis; orange: spliceosome and pale green: RIG-I-like receptor signaling pathway.
