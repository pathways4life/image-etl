Figure 1. From: POLTERGEIST and PLL1 are required for stem cell function with potential roles in cell asymmetry and auxin signaling.

(At left) A model for CLV pathway signaling at the shoot meristem. Apically-generated CLV3 signal primarily activates CLV1 on the apical face of L3 stem cells within the shoot meristem. CLV1 in turn negatively regulates POL/PLL1 primarily on the apical portion of the cell. Upon periclinal division, the asymmetric distribution of POL/PLL1 drives apical/basal fates, including the activation of WUS in the basal daughter.
