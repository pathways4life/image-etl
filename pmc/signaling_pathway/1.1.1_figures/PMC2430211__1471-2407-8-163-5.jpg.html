Figure 5. From: Involvement of TSC genes and differential expression of other members of the mTOR signaling pathway in oral squamous cell carcinoma.

Expression of other members of the mTOR signaling pathway in oral tumors. a) mRNA expression of 10 genes in 16 matched normal and tumor samples. Horizontal lines represent mean values of mRNA expression across normal or tumor samples. b) Western blot analysis of matched normal (N) and tumor (T) samples from eight patients. Numbers represent patient numbers. Blots were probed with antibodies for anti-AKT, anti-p-AKT (Thr308), anti-p70S6K1 and anti-p-70S6K1 (Thr389).
