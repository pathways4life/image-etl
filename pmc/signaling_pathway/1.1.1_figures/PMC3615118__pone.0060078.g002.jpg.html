Figure 2. LPS-liposomes induce the TRIF-dependent signaling pathway but not the MyD88-dependent signaling pathway.. From: Liposomal Lipopolysaccharide Initiates TRIF-Dependent Signaling Pathway Independent of CD14.

Thioglycollate-elicited peritoneal macrophages (1×106 cells) from WT mice were stimulated with LPS (100 ng/mL) or LPS-liposomes (100 ng/mL) for 0–120 min. The cells were then lysed and the extracts immunoblotted with anti-IRF3 (A), anti-IκBα antibodies (B), or anti-IRAK and anti-MAPK antibodies (C). Data are average of three independent experiments and band intensity was quantified with Image J 1.45. The values represent means ± S.E.M *P<0.05.
