Figure 6. Genes induced by INZ in p53 pathway.. From: Global Effect of Inauhzin on Human p53-Responsive Transcriptome.

The p53 signaling pathway identified by Pathway-Express analysis. Pathway-Express analysis was performed on the INZ-up-regulated genes.
