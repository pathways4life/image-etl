Figure 3. From: NMDA receptor activation stimulates transcription-independent rapid wnt5a protein synthesis via the MAPK signaling pathway.

mTOR signaling pathway is not required for the NMDAR-dependent Wnt5a protein synthesis. Primary cortical neurons (10 DIV) were pre-treated with vehicle (Control) or 25nΜ Rapamycin for 30 min, followed by addition of 50 μΜ NMDA for 15 min. Western blotting analysis of Wnt5a and phosphor-P70S6K proteins were performed. Graphs (mean ± SEM) are from four independent experiments (*, p < 0.05; **, p < 0.01; #, p > 0.05; One-way ANOVA).
