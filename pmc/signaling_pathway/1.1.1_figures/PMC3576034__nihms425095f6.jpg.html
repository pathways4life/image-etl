Fig. 6. From: Deciphering the Mechanisms of Tumorigenesis in Human Pancreatic Ductal Epithelial Cells.

Ingenuity Pathway Analysis (IPA) signaling pathway of microarray data and the proposed model for transformation of the HPDE cell line. A, IPA canonical signaling pathway analysis for transformation. Bar indicates significance. The length of the bar indicates only that the differentially expressed proteins are related to this pathway but does not indicate upregulation or downregulation of the pathway. B, IPA biological function analysis for transformation. C, Proposed model for transformation of the HPDE cell line.
