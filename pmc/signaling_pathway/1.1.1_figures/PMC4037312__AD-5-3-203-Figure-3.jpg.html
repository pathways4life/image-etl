Schematic representation of PI3K/AKT/PTEN signaling, which modulate NOXs-derived ROS. NOXs sense hypoxia and can help to mediate activation of angiogenic pathway, while maintaining unregulated cell growth and apoptotic cell death. Examples of molecules known to act on the regulatory pathways are shown. Note that some critical pathways have been omitted for clarity.
