Figure 3. TGF-β signaling pathway impaired mice survive shorter than WT mice under alcohol- and LPS-treatment. From: Generation of a mouse model of T-cell lymphoma based on chronic LPS challenge and TGF-? signaling disruption.

Mice with constitutive disruption of the TGF-β signaling via heterozygous deletion of β2-Spectrin (Sptbn1+/−) (n=14), Smad3 (Smad3+/−) (n=9) orboth (Sptbn1+/−; Smad3+/−) (n=11) treated with alcohol and LPS have significantly decreased cancer-free survival compared to wild type mice (n=15). *p<0.05/ ***p<0.005.
