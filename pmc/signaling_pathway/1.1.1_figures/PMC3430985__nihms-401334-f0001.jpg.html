Figure 1. Bacterial Effector Proteins Target Conserved Eukaryotic Signaling Pathways. From: The Yeast Saccharomyces cerevisiae: A Versatile Model System for the Identification and Characterization of Bacterial Virulence Proteins.

The intersection of a MAPK signaling pathway and Rho-GTPase cycling is illustrated along with the effector proteins from bacteria that target these pathways. Effector proteins with functions that mimic a mammalian protein are displayed in blue, while those that modify and deactivate mammalian proteins are displayed in red.
