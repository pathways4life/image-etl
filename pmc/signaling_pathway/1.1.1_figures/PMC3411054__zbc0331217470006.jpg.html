FIGURE 6. From: Targeting the Sonic Hedgehog Pathway in Keratocystic Odontogenic Tumor.

The protein expression of SHH pathway components was down-regulated by cyclopamine treatment. KCOT-1 cells were treated with cyclopamine (C) (10, 20, and 25 μm) or tomatidine (T) (5 and 10 μm) or no treatment (NT) for 48 h. A, protein expression for the SHH pathway. B, densitometric analysis of the calculated reduction of the protein levels of the SHH signaling pathway. OD Ratio, optical density ratio.
