Figure 2. From: IL-7 inhibits fibroblast TGF-? production and signaling in pulmonary fibrosis.

IL-7R signaling requires an intact JAK1/STAT1 pathway. U4A, U3A, and U4A/JAK1 fibroblast cell lines were cultured in medium containing variable doses of recombinant IL-7 for a 24-hour incubation. Culture supernatants were assayed for TGF-β by ELISA. Whereas TGF-β production remained unchanged in the U4A and U3A cells, IL-7 decreased TGF-β production in a dose-dependent manner in U4A/JAK1 cells (P < 0.01 at 50–400 ng/ml IL-7).
