Figure 4. Gene expression of Wnt signaling pathway regulators:. From: Impact of Laminitis on the Canonical Wnt Signaling Pathway in Basal Epithelial Cells of the Equine Digital Laminae.

Expression of genes encoding A - Wnt4, B - FZD4, C - LRP6, D - Dsh, E - PP1, F - Axin1, G - APC, H - CKIα, I - GSK3β relative to the gene expression of GapDH. Horizontal lines indicate mean ± standard error of mean of n = 8 CON, n = 6 OG1, and n = 5 OG3, animals. * = p<0.05, ** = p<0.01, as calculated by one-way ANOVA.
