Figure 4. From: THE EFFECT OF PROPRANOLOL ON GENE EXPRESSION DURING THE BLOOD ALCOHOL CYCLE OF RATS FED ETHANOL INTRAGASTRICALLY.

KEGG functional pathways that were changed by feeding ethanol+propranolol compared with control rats are shown. The most pathways affected were the cytokine-cytokine receptor interaction, Jak-STAT signaling pathway and the Mapk signaling pathway.
