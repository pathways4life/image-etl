Fig 8. Both Notch signaling and proteasome pathway contributes to the osteogenesis of hUC-MSCs.. From: The Notch Signaling Regulates CD105 Expression, Osteogenic Differentiation and Immunomodulation of Human Umbilical Cord Mesenchymal Stem Cells.

The semi-quantitative RT-PCR shows that the treatment with 2.5 Î¼M GSI-I significantly reduces RGC32 transcription at day 1 and day 3 after osteogenic induction (A). However, whereas 5 nM Bortezomib alone reduces RGC32 transcription at day 3, not day 1, of osteogenic induction, and siNotch transfection alone slightly reduces RGC32 transcription at both day 1 and day 3, the combination of both siNotch1 transfection and Bortezomib treatment significantly reduces the transcription at both day 1 and day 3 after osteogenic induction (B).
