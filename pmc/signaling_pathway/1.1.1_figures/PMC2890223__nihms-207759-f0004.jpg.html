Figure 4. From: NCX-4016, a nitro-derivative of aspirin, inhibits EGFR and STAT3 signaling and modulates Bcl-2 proteins in cisplatin-resistant human ovarian cancer cells and xenografts.

Effect of NCX-4016 on EGFR/PI3K and STAT3 signaling in CR ovarian cancer cells. Representative immunoblot images showing the expression levels of pEGFR, pAkt and pSTAT3 and their phosphorylated counterparts are shown. The cells were treated with NCX-4016 (100 Î¼M) for 12 or 24 h. The results show inhibition of EGFR and STAT3 activation, but no effect on MAPK pathway, in the CR cells treated with NCX-4016.
