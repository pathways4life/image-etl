Figure 7. TGF-β signaling in EMT program in thyroid cells.. From: Cadherin 6 Is a New RUNX2 Target in TGF-? Signalling Pathway.

Schematic representation of the TGF-β dependent EMT program in thyroid cells. Malignant transformation of thyroid cells is accompanied by a constitutive activation of the TGF-β signaling pathway. TGF-β cascade controls the expression of a number of transcription factors (EMT-TF) including RUNX2 that alter the gene expression profile of the cells to activate the EMT program.
