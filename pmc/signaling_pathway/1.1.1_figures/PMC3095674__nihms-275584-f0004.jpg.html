Figure 4. CsA activates TGFβ activated kinase 1 (TAK1) signaling pathway. From: Procarcinogenic Effects of Cyclosporine A are Mediated through the Activation of TAK1/TAB1 Signaling Pathway.

Summary of the cascades of events triggered by CsA. .CsA enhances TAK1 by activating TGFβ-dependent signaling . CsA also augments the expression of upstream kinases ZMP and AMPK in addition to IRAK. The enhancement in the expression/activity of these signaling proteins contributes to MAP kinase and NFkB activation, which together triggers proliferation, reduced apoptosis and enhanced angiogenesis.
