Fig. 3. From: Notch Signaling Proteins: Legitimate Targets for Cancer Therapy.

Diagram of putative therapeutic target in the Notch pathway. Notch signaling could be inhibited theoretically at many different levels. It is possible to (1) interfere with Notch-ligand interactions, (2) inhibit receptor activation, (3) promote Notch ubiquitination and degradation, (4) inhibit its translocation to the nuclear compartment and (5) inhibit NICD nuclear complex formation.
