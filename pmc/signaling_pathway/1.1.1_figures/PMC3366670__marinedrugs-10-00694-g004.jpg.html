Figure 4. From: A Marine Anthraquinone SZ-685C Overrides Adriamycin-Resistance in Breast Cancer Cells through Suppressing Akt Signaling.

Effect of SZ-685C on the Akt signaling pathway in MCF-7/ADR and MCF-7/Akt cells. Cells were treated with different concentrations of ADR (A) and SZ-685C (B) for 48 h as noted. Western blotting analysis was performed using antibodies against phospho-Akt (Ser473 or Thr308), total Akt, phospho-Bad (Ser 136), total Bad, and Bcl-xL, with Actin used as a loading control.
