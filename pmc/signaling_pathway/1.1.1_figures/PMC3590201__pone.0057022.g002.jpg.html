Figure 2. KEGG pathway map for TGF-beta signaling pathway.. From: The Identification of Pathway Markers in Intracranial Aneurysm Using Genome-Wide Association Data from Two Different Populations.

The shade of red color in genes indicates the number of targeted SNPs in JP population per base pair of the gene. Red refers to the highest targeted gene, whereas white refers to a gene product, not targeted by the SNPs. Blue border indicates that the gene is found to be differentially expressed.
