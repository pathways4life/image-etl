Figure 3. From: miR-218 modulate hepatocellular carcinoma cell proliferation through PTEN/AKT/PI3K pathway and HoxA10.

miR-218 regulated PTEN/AKT/PI3K pathway in HCC. (A) MHC97L cells were transfected with miR-218-mimics and miR-218-inhibitor, along with their corresponding control siRNAs. Western blotting analysis was used to examine the cell cycle proteins Cyclin D1 and p21, and (B) PTEN/AKT/PI3K signaling pathway related proteins, including AKT/phosphorylated-AKT, GSK-3β phosphorylated-GSK-3β PTEN.
