Figure 4. Crucial signaling pathways activated by various ligands and the regulatory networks regulating cell fate decisions of SSCs and differentiating spermatogonia. From: Signaling molecules and pathways regulating the fate of spermatogonial stem cells.

GDNF and SCF activate both the Ras/Erk and PI3K/Akt pathways, whereas SCF cannot signal via JAK/STAT pathway. GDNF can activate Bcl6b, Lhx1, and ERM, but not PLZF or TAF4b. BMP4 signaling via Smad4/5 pathway and Upd (Unpaired) signaling through JAK/STAT pathway are also shown. Arrows indicated “activation”, whereas the blocked arrows denote “inactivation”.
