Figure 4. Hsp90 does not repress Ras1-PKA signaling via negative regulators of the pathway. From: Hsp90 Orchestrates Temperature-Dependent Candida albicans Morphogenesis Via Ras1-PKA Signaling.

Loss of function of negative regulators of the Ras1-PKA cascade did not phenocopy Hsp90 inhibition while inhibition of Hsp90 induced filamentation. Cells were incubated in YPD at 30°C for 6 hours with GdA, as indicated. Scale bar, 10 μm.
