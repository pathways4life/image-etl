Figure 5. From: PCI-24781 down-regulates EZH2 expression and then promotes glioma apoptosis by suppressing the PIK3K/Akt/mTOR pathway.

EZH2 gene silencing attenuated the expression of proteins involved in the PI3K/Akt signaling pathway. M – blank control (mock), NC – normal control (cells with scrambled siRNA), SIR – cells treated with specific siRNA. The blots are representative of 3 experiments.
