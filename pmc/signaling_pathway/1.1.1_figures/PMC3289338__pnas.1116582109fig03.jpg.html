Fig. 3. From: Structural reorganization of the interleukin-7 signaling complex.

Proposed signaling mechanism of the IL-7 pathway. Signaling model of IL-7Rα–IL-7–γc interactions indicating preassembly of the IL-7Rα homodimer and IL-7Rα–γc heterodimer from cell biological studies (12, 13). There are no structures of the IL-7Rα–γc heterodimer or the IL-7Rα–IL-7–γc ternary complex. The distance between the D2 domains of IL-7Rα and γc was an average of the γc structures with IL-2 [PDB ID code 2bfi (20); PDB ID code 2erj (29)] and IL-4 [PDB ID code 3bpl (23)].
