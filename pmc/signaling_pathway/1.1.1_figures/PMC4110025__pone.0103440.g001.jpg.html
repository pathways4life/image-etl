Figure 1. Effects of HPV-16 oncoproteins on HIF-1α expression and PI3K/Akt/mTOR signaling pathway activation in NSCLC cells.. From: Roles of PI3K/Akt and c-Jun Signaling Pathways in Human Papillomavirus Type 16 Oncoprotein-Induced HIF-1?, VEGF, and IL-8 Expression and In Vitro Angiogenesis in Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer Cells.

(A) Western blot analysis of HIF-1α protein levels in stable-transfected A549 cells. (B) Real-time PCR analysis of HIF-1α mRNA levels in stable-transfected A549 cells. (C and D) Western blot analysis of p-Akt, p-P70S6K, p-P85S6K, and p-mTOR protein levels in transfected A549 (C) and NCI-H460 (D) cells.
