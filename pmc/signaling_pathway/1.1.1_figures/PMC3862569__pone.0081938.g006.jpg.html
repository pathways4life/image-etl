Figure 6. Parallel comparison of BR biosynthetic and signaling pathways.. From: Brassinosteroids Regulate Plant Growth through Distinct Signaling Pathways in Selaginella and Arabidopsis.

(A) Pathway showing the order in which Arabidopsis BR biosynthetic CYPs function. Selaginella CYP90s are shown to the right. (B) Model comparing Arabidopsis (left) with Selaginella (right) signaling pathways. Abbreviations are given beneath Table 2. P indicates phosphorylation. Solid lines indicate established signaling routes, whereas broken lines show possible routes. Flat-tipped and pointed arrows indicate inhibitory and positive actions, respectively. Question marks indicate steps that remain to be characterized.
