Figure 2. From: Modulation of myocardial contraction by peroxynitrite.

Pathophysiological signaling pathway of high peroxynitrite. This is a diagrammatic representation of high peroxynitrite signaling and various proposed end targets within the cardiomyocyte. The endogenous production of high levels of peroxynitrite can occur via NOS2/NADPH oxidase/xanthine oxidase.
