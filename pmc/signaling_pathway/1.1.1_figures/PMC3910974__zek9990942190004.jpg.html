FIG 4. From: Sck1 Negatively Regulates Gpa2-Mediated Glucose Signaling in Schizosaccharomyces pombe.

Pathway model showing two distinct roles for Sck1 in nutrient signaling. Work described here and elsewhere suggests that Sck1 is both a negative regulator of cAMP signaling via Gpa2 regulation and a kinase that acts in parallel to PKA, possibly as a sensor of nitrogen levels. Gpa2 activation of PKA is via the activation of adenylyl cyclase (Git2/Cyr1; not shown).
