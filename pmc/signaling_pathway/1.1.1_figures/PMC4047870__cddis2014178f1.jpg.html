Figure 1. From: Rapamycin reverses insulin resistance (IR) in high-glucose medium without causing IR in normoglycemic medium.

The insulin/mTOR/HIF-1 pathway: a simplified schema. Glucose activates mTOR, which in turn blocks insulin signaling (a feedback loop). The mTOR pathway increases translation of the HIF-1α RNA. However, accumulation of the HIF-1α protein is tightly limited by its degradation via a PHD-feedback loop under normoxia.28, 66 Red lines, inhibitory feedback loops
