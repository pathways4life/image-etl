Figure 4. Western blot analysis of VEGFR2 downstream signaling transduction.. From: MiR-200c Increases the Radiosensitivity of Non-Small-Cell Lung Cancer Cell Line A549 by Targeting VEGF-VEGFR2 Pathway.

Ectopic expression of miR-200c inhibited phosphorylation of Akt and ERK1/2, inhibition of miR-200c increased activation of Akt and ERK1/2 pathway. Each experiment was repeated three times.
