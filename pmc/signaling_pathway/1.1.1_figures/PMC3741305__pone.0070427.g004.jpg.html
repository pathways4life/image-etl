Figure 4. Proposed model for the contribution of EGFR/Ras/MEK signaling pathway to TGF-β1-induced stemness and tumorigenicity.. From: EMT-Induced Stemness and Tumorigenicity Are Fueled by the EGFR/Ras Pathway.

The activation of TGF-β pathway triggers Runx3−/−p53−/− GIF-14 gastric epithelial cells into EMT, concurrently inducing a stem cell-like and tumorigenic state. The data presented in this report show that TGF-β1 induces an EGFR/Ras gene expression signature marked by increased Egfr expression, which sensitized GIF-14 cells to EGF. The activation of EGFR/Ras pathway promotes stemness and tumorigenicity in GIF-14 cells in a MEK1/2-dependent manner that did not involve increased EMT.
