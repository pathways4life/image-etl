Figure 5. From: TLR9 signaling repressed tumor suppressor miR-7 expression through up-regulation of HuR in human lung cancer cells.

TLR9 signaling induced the expression of HuR through Akt pathway. 95D cells were treated with 50 μM PI3K inhibitor (LY294002) or specific MEK (Mitogen-activated protein kinase) inhibitor (U0126), 2.5 μM Akt inhibitor IV respectively, and then cultured in 24-well plate in the presence of 10 ug/ml CpG ODNs. 72 hrs later, the expression of HuR protein was determined by western blot and calculated (A). (B) Schematic diagram for TLR9 signaling induced expression of HuR.
