Figure 4. From: Identification of links between small molecules and miRNAs in human cancers based on transcriptional responses.

(a) miR-23b and tretinoin, which was associated with this miRNA in thyroid cancer, shared the common target genes, ALDH1A1 and ALDH1A2. (b) The MAPK signaling pathway that was enriched with the common targets of miR-19a, miR-19b and miR-23b. The red nodes represented these common targets. (c) The mTOR signaling pathway that was enriched with the common targets of miR-19a, miR-19b and miR-23b. The red nodes represented these common targets.
