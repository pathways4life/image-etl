Figure 3. From: A Bioinformatics Resource for TWEAK-Fn14 Signaling Pathway.

TWEAK signaling pathway: illustration of the TWEAK signaling pathway as visualized in NetSlim web page. Each molecule is linked to its corresponding page in NetPath. Each reaction is linked to its respective PubMed citation. Dashed arrows represent the downstream reactions leading to the corresponding events while the solid arrows indicate the direct association between the indicated molecules. Gene symbol(s) has been used to denote proteins in the pathway map (refer to Synonymous for common names).
