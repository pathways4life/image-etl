Figure 11. From: Advanced Glycation End-Products Induce Tubular CTGF via TGF-?-Independent Smad3 Signaling.

A schematic diagram of AGE-induced CTGF expression is shown. Engagement of RAGE by AGEs activates Smad signaling via both the ERK/p38 MAPK-Smad signaling cross-talk pathway (TGF-β–independent pathway) and the classic TGF-β signaling pathway (TGF-β–dependent pathway) to induce CTGF expression.
