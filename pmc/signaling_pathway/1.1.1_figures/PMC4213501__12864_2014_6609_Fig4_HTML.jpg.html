Figure 4. From: Differential transcriptomic analyses revealed genes and signaling pathways involved in iono-osmoregulation and cellular remodeling in the gills of euryhaline Mozambique tilapia, Oreochromis mossambicus.

Canonical signaling pathways that were significantly (Benjamini-Hochberg adjusted P-value < 0.05) enriched with putative human homolog proteins encoded by the assembled contiq sequences used in the analysis. Proportion (%) of the signaling pathway being represented by the human homolog encoded proteins is been indicated on the x-axis. Refer to Additional file for complete list.
