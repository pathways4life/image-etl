Fig 4. sAPP stimulates the Notch signaling pathway during gliogenesis. From: Involvement of notch signaling pathway in amyloid precursor protein induced glial differentiation.

(A) Treatment of sAPP (100ng/ml) promoted GFAP expression as well as NICD generation. (B) and (C) sAPP induced generation of NICD in a time- and dose- dependent manner as indicated above. Generation of NICD was measured by Western blot analysis using anti-active Notch Ab. (D) sAPP induced NICD generation was suppressed by neutralizing antibody (22C11). One-factor ANOVA followed by post hoc analysis (Student-Newman-Keuls) was used to analyze the differences between experimental groups and control groups (*: p<0.05, **: p<0.01).
