Figure 5. Glucose modulates ribosome-related modules through glucose response pathway.. From: Statistical Analysis Reveals Co-Expression Patterns of Many Pairs of Genes in Yeast Are Jointly Regulated by Interacting Loci.

The regulatory loci in the modules contain genes involved in the glucose response pathway (red nodes). This indicates how the glucose condition modulates the co-expression pattern between ribosome genes (blue nodes) and glucose signaling or metabolic genes (green nodes).
