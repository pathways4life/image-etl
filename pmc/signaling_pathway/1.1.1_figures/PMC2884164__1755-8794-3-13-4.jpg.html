Figure 4. From: Signaling pathway networks mined from human pituitary adenoma proteomics data.

NRF2-mediated oxidative-stress response pathway that is involved with pituitary adenoma protein-mapping data. A gray label denotes an identified protein. The various shapes of nodes denote the different functions. A duplicated shape means this node contains multiple components. An arrow denotes the pathway direction. A line with a small circle denotes a biological result.
