Figure 6. From: SD118-Xanthocillin X (1), a Novel Marine Agent Extracted from Penicillium commune, Induces Autophagy through the Inhibition of the MEK/ERK Pathway.

SD118-Xanthocillin X (1) is involved in the autophagy signaling pathway. Effect of SD118-xanthocillin X (1) on the MEK/ERK/mTOR pathway. HepG2 cells were treated with 24.3 µM SD118-xanthocillin X (1), harvested and subjected to western blot analysis, which shows the time-dependent reduction of p-ERK1/2, p-mTOR and Bcl2. Protein contents were normalized with β-actin.
