FIG. 7. From: Hyperglycemia-Induced Protein Kinase C ?2 Activation Induces Diastolic Cardiac Dysfunction in Diabetic Rats by Impairing Caveolin-3 Expression and Akt/eNOS Signaling.

Schematic depicting hyperglycemia-induced PKCβ2 activation effects upon Cav-3–modulated Akt/eNOS signaling pathway. Cardiomyocyte caveolae are required for hyperglycemia-induced PKCβ2 activation (translocation from cytosol to caveolae membrane). Excessive PKCβ2 activation decreased Cav-3 expression, impairing the Akt/eNOS signaling pathway. Solid arrows depict stimulation, while transverse “T” shape indicates inhibition. PI3K, phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase.
