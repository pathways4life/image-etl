Figure 8. From: Development of kinomic analyses to identify dysregulated signaling pathways in cells expressing cytoplasmic PrP.

The levels of proteins in the Akt1/p70S6K branch of the mTOR signaling pathway change coordinately. Time-course analyses of the normalized expression levels of Akt1, mTOR, p70S6K, S6, eIF4B, and eEF2 in N2a cells expressing CyPrPEGFP for 12, 24, or 48 h. Mean ± SD; n = 3.
