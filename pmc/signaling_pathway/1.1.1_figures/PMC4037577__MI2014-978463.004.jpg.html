Figure 4. From: Obesity, Inflammation, and Lung Injury (OILI): The Good.

Signaling pathway of SFRP5, a decoy for WNT signaling pathway, which further activates β-catenin and then JNK. Activated JNK promotes proinflammatory cytokines TNFα, IL-6, and MCP-1. Under obese state, the production of SFRP5 was reduced and thus the decoying effect was weak, which is translated into the increased proinflammation and insulin resistance.
