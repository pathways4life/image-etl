Fig. 8. A conceptual view of the mechanism of IFN-γ-induced apoptosis. From: Induction of indoleamine 2,3-dioxygenase by interferon-gamma in human lens epithelial cells: Apoptosis through the formation of 3-hydroxykynurenine.

IFN-γ induces IDO via JAK-STAT1 signaling, which catalyzes the first step in the kynurenine pathway. This pathway produces 3OHKyn, which reacts and chemically modifies proteins and also generates ROS, resulting in apoptosis.
