Figure 8. Hypothetical model of 16K hPRL effects on Notch signaling in endothelial/pericyte interactions.. From: The Antiangiogenic 16K Prolactin Impairs Functional Tumor Neovascularization by Inhibiting Vessel Maturation.

When endothelial cells are cultured with pericytes, Dll4/Notch4 pathway is activated. 16K hPRL treatment (6 h) alters Dll4/Notch4 signaling by decreasing DLL4, NOTCH4, HEY2 and EPHRINB2 transcripts in endothelial cells. After 16 h of endothelial cell-pericyte cocultures, Notch3 and Dll1 are increased respectively in pericytes and endothelial cells. 16K hPRL inhibits the Dll1/Notch3 signaling by reducing DLL1 transcripts in endothelial cells and NOTCH3 and aSMA transcripts in pericytes.
