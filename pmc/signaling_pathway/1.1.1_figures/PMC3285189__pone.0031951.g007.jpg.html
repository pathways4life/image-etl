Figure 7. Stimulation of the H2 receptor inhibited the ERK signaling pathway.. From: Histamine Derived from Probiotic Lactobacillus reuteri Suppresses TNF via Modulation of PKA and ERK Signaling.

Immunoblot studies were performed to determine the effect of histamine on activation of the ERK signaling pathway. ERK was activated by phosphorylation on Thr202/Tyr204 (P-ERK). Treatment of TLR-stimulated THP-1 cells with L. reuteri 6475, histamine, or U0126 suppressed activation of ERK1/2, an effect that was significantly blocked in the presence of ranitidine. The Î²-actin antibody served as the protein loading control.
