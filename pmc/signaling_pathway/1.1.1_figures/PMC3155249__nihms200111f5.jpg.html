Figure 5. Glucocorticoid (GC)/Insulin signaling strongly correlates with MSI in the CA region. From: Aging-Related Gene Expression in Hippocampus Proper compared to Dentate Gyrus is Selectively Associated with Metabolic Syndrome Variables in Rhesus Monkeys.

Six aging related genes associated with GC/stress and/or insulin/IGF1 signaling were used to construct a GC/insulin ratio that increases with elevated GC function and/or decreased insulin signaling. Upper: Expression values for the 6 genes were averaged by subject (note: Grif, a downregulated repressor in the glucocorticoid pathway and Akt3, a downregulated effector of the insulin pathway (*) were inverted prior to averaging). The resulting GC/insulin ratio values correlated strongly (r = 0.9; Pearson's test) with the MSI. Lower: names and aliases of populating genes, as well as protein names and basic functions are listed.
