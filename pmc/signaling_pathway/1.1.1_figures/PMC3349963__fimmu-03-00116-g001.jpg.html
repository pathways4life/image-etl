Figure 1. From: IL-13 Signaling in Liver Fibrogenesis.

(A) Canonical and non-canonical IL-13 signaling in cells. IL-13 uses JAK/STAT6 pathway to transduce its canonical signaling in cells. In addition, IL-13 activates non-canonical STAT3, Erk1/2, etc., pathways in some settings. (B) Non-canonical signaling pathways of IL-13 play a critical role in liver fibrosis. IL-13 interacts with TGF-Î² signaling pathway to induce CTGF expression in HSCs via Erk1/2 pathway (Liu et al., 2011).
