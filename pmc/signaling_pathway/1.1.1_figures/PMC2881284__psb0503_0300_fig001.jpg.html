Figure 1. From: Multiple receptor complexes assembled for transmitting CLV3 signaling in Arabidopsis.

Hypothetic CLV3 signaling pathway model. As it is shown, CLV3 might be transmitted via at least three receptor complexes. The first one is that CLV1 monomer or CLV1 homodimers. The second one is that CLV2-CRN heterodimers forming a tetramer through CRN-CRN interaction. In addition, CLV1, CLV2 and CRN may form complexes as the third and functional receptor complex.
