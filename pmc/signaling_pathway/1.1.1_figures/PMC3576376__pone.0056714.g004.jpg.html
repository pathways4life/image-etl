Figure 4. PCA pathway analysis of FLT3 ITD AML samples and healthy BMMb compared to FLT3 WT AML.. From: Functional Pathway Analysis Using SCNP of FLT3 Receptor Pathway Deregulation in AML Provides Prognostic Information Independent from Mutational Status.

PCA analysis of FLT3L-induced signaling (PC 1) and AraC/Daunorubicin-induced apoptosis measured by cPARP (PC 2) in healthy BMMb (blue dots), FLT3 ITD (red dots) and FLT3 WT (green dots). Donors with low FLT3 ITD mutational load (<40%) are indicated by arrows.
