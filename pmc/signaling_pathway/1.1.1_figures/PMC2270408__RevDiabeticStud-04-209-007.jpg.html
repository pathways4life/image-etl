Figure 6. Involvement of Foxo1 in the nucleo-cytoplasmic translocation of PDX-1 which is induced by oxidative stress and the subsequent activation of the JNK pathway. From: Pleiotropic Roles of PDX-1 in the Pancreas.

Oxidative stress and the subsequent activation of the JNK pathway induce nuclear translocation of Foxo1 through modification of insulin signaling in Î²-cells, which leads to the nucleo-cytoplasmic translocation of PDX-1 and reduction of its DNA binding activity.
