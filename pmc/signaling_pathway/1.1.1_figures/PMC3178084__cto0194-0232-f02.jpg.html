Fig. 2. From: Runx2 Regulates the Gene Network Associated with Insulin Signaling and Energy Homeostasis.

Runx2-deficient cells exhibit suppression of genes by insulin at multiple stages of the signaling pathway. Proteins at different stages of the pathway from the receptor, transducer, nuclear effecter, and target gene are shown. Expressions of respective genes are downregulated upon addition of insulin to Runx2-null cells (indicated by red arrows), while insulin robustly induces these genes in 3T3-L1 cells (indicated by green arrows).
