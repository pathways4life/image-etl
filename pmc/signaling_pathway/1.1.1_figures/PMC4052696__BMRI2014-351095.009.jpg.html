Analyzing the observations comprehensively on modular study and referring to related literature, the signaling pathway from TGFβ1 targeting OCIAD2 was concluded as shown in Figure 9: the signaling transmits from TGFβ1- TGFβR1- AR-OCIAD2 in liver cancer mesenchymal stem cell, then differentiates into Tumor-Associated-Fibroblasts (TAFs) in tumor stroma.
