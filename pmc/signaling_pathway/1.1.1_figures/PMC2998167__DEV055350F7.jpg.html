Fig. 7. From: Regulation of C. elegans presynaptic differentiation and neurite branching via a novel signaling pathway initiated by SAM-10.

Model of SAM-10 signaling pathway. SAM-10 is translocated into the nucleus in an LDB-1-dependent manner. In the nucleus, SAM-10 regulates PLM synaptic differentiation and neurite branch positioning, presumably by regulating gene expression. SAM-10 regulation of synaptic differentiation is mediated at least partially by suppressing prk-2 transcription.
