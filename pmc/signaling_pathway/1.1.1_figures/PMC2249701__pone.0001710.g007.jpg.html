Figure 7. Schematic model for a mechanism of Sirt1 activating ERK/S6K1 signaling pathway involved in cell senescence.. From: SIRT1 Overexpression Antagonizes Cellular Senescence with Activated ERK/S6k1 Signaling in Human Diploid Fibroblasts.

The activation of SIRT1 in human lung fibroblasts suppressed p16INK4A/Rb pathway and provoked ERK/S6K1 signaling, which, in turn, may inhibit p16INK4A/Rb signaling via Id1 induction.
