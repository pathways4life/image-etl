Figure 7. From: ProNGF induces PTEN via p75NTR to suppress Trk -mediated survival signaling in brain neurons.

Schematic diagram of the interaction of p75NTR and TrkB signaling. Apoptosis induced by proNGF via p75NTR requires suppression of the TrkB-induced PI3 kinase pathway by PTEN. When PTEN is inhibited, BDNF-induced activation of Akt can inhibit apoptosis.
