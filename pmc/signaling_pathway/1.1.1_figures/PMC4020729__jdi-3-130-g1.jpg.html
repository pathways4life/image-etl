Figure 1. From: GLUT12: a second insulin-responsive glucose transporters as an emerging target for type 2 diabetes.

 Proposed signaling pathway regulating GLUT12 translocation and glucose transport. The insulin receptor phosphorylates IRS1, which activates PI3K and downstream putative effectors that modulate steps in the GLUT12 trafficking pathways. Activation of PI3K signaling is required for insulin‐stimulated GLUT12 translocation to the plasma membrane.
