Inhibiting Aurora-B expression decreases the activity of the PI3K/Akt/NF-κB signaling pathway. With the downregulation of Aurora-B, the p-Akt, Akt, MMP-2, MMP-9 and NF-κB protein expression levels decreased significantly in the cells transfected with MiR-Aurora-B when compared with the cells transfected with MiR-Neg.
