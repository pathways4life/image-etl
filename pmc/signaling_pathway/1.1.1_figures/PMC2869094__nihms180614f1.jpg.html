Figure 1. Selected gene interactions and epigenetic dysregulation of the canonical WNT pathway. From: Epigenetic regulation of WNT signaling in chronic lymphocytic leukemia.

The genes shown in solid black have previously been reported to be dysregulated through DNA methylation, histone modifications, miRNA expression and combinations of these mechanisms. How they affect the overall signaling pathway is not yet known.LEF: Lymphoid enhancing factor; SFRP: Soluble frizzled protein.
