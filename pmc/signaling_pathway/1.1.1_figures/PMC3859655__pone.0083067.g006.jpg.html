Figure 6. Schematic diagram of a negative regulatory loop of BMP-miR-17 family-BMPRII.. From: Role of miR-17 Family in the Negative Feedback Loop of Bone Morphogenetic Protein Signaling in Neuron.

BMPs activate Smads via BMPRI-II complex, leading to the increase of miR-17 family, which represses BMPRII expression to prevent aberrant activation of this signaling pathway.
