Figure 7. VEGFA expression is regulated by ER stress.. From: Transcriptional Regulation of VEGF-A by the Unfolded Protein Response Pathway.

ER stress signaling regulates VEGFA expression via its principal regulators Ire1α, Perk and ATF6α. Proposed pathway shows how ER stress signaling might affect growth and survival of both developing placenta as well as tumor through angiogenesis. This is accomplished by activating VEGFA transcription via different regulatory regions on the VEGFA gene.
