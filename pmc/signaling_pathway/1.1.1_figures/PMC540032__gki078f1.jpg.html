Figure 1. From: The PANTHER database of protein families, subfamilies, functions and pathways.

CellDesigner (6) diagram of the insulin/IGF receptor signaling pathway. Proteins (blue and brown boxes) are mapped onto PANTHER HMMs. Active forms (dashed-line boxes) and phosphorylated forms (small circles around the letter ‘P’) of proteins are clearly indicated in the diagram. Over 60 pathways (mostly signaling pathways) are currently available.
