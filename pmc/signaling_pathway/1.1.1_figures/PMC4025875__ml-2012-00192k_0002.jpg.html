Figure 2. From: Structure-Activity Relationships for Side Chain Oxysterol Agonists of the Hedgehog Signaling Pathway.

Activation of the Hh signaling pathway. The relative expression of GLI1 and PTCH was determined in M2-10B4 cells following treatment with the standard Hh activating OHCs (1:2) or 35 and the Hh-specific inhibitor Cyc. Cells were pretreated with Cyc (5 μM) for 2 h and then treated with OHC (5 μM). mRNA expression was determined following 24 h of incubation. Data are from a representative experiment performed in triplicate.
