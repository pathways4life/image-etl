The two main signaling pathways downstream the IGF-IR are both involved in the regulation of transcription factors of the Twist, Snail, and ZEB1 families, all involved in the EMT program. The IRS-1/PI3K/Akt pathway leads to the phosphorylation and inactivation of GSK3β, thus preventing the phosphorylation and proteasome targeting of Snail and Slug.
