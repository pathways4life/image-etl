Figure 7. Model for the effect of Mop on EGFR endocytosis. From: Myopic acts in the endocytic pathway to enhance signaling by the Drosophila EGF receptor.

Upon activation of the EGFR, ubiquitination by Cbl induces EGFR internalization through clathrin-coated vesicles. These vesicles fuse with early endosomes and the EGFR is passed from the Hrs complex to the ESCRT-I, ESCRT-II and ESCRT-III complexes as the endosomes are transformed into multivesicular bodies (MVBs).
